# 实用英文语块（原文例句+音标+同义词/替换用法）
## 一、日常交际&礼貌用语
1. **permission to do sth**
   释义：允许做某事
   例句：Permission to come aboard allowed?
   音标：/pərˈmɪʃn tu kʌm əˈbɔːrd/
   替换：May I...? / Can I...? / be allowed to do sth

2. **thank you for doing sth**
   释义：感谢你做某事
   例句：Thank you for bringing me home.
   替换：I appreciate you doing... / Thanks for... / be grateful for...

3. **it has been my great honour to do sth**
   释义：做某事是我的莫大荣幸
   例句：It has been my great honour and joy to know you.
   替换：It’s a privilege to... / I’m honored to... / It’s my pleasure to...

## 二、职场/工作通用
1. **have the conn**
   释义：接管负责、主持工作
   例句：Scotty, you have the conn.
   音标：/hæv ðə kɑːn/
   替换：take charge / take over / be in command

2. **report to sb**
   释义：向某人报到、汇报
   例句：Report to the bridge.
   替换：report in to sb / check in with sb / brief sb

3. **be rated for sth**
   释义：具备某岗位资质
   例句：You’re not rated for helm as well as navigation.
   替换：be qualified for sth / be certified for sth / be eligible for sth

4. **return to duty**
   释义：返岗、恢复工作
   例句：Then I can return to duty?
   替换：get back to work / resume duty / go back to one’s post

5. **conduct business**
   释义：处理事务、洽谈工作
   例句：We should conduct our business face to face.
   替换：handle affairs / do business / manage matters

## 三、应急&行动指令
1. **stand down**
   释义：解除戒备、退下、停止行动
   例句：Klingon vessel, stand down or be fired upon!
   音标：/stænd daʊn/
   替换：back off / cease action / stand by

2. **hold on**
   释义：坚持住、稍等
   例句：Hold on. I’m going to try something.
   替换：hang on / wait a moment / stick it out

3. **prepare to do sth**
   释义：准备做某事
   例句：Prepare to beam aboard.
   替换：get ready to do sth / be poised to do sth / plan to do sth

4. **target locked on**
   释义：锁定目标（职场可指锁定问题/对象）
   例句：Hot and locked on the Klingon.
   替换：target acquired / locked onto the target / fix on the target

## 四、情感&状态表达
1. **feel fine**
   释义：感觉还好、身体无碍
   例句：Doctor, I feel fine.
   替换：feel all right / feel good / be in good condition

2. **be ashamed of sth**
   释义：为某事感到羞愧
   例句：Are you ashamed of our record?
   替换：feel guilty about sth / be embarrassed by sth / regret sth

3. **it’s worth it**
   释义：这是值得的
   例句：It was worth it. It was all worth it.
   替换：it pays off / it’s worthwhile / it’s well worth it

4. **a steady stream of**
   释义：源源不断的……
   例句：There's been a steady stream of visitors through here today.
   替换：a constant flow of / an endless stream of / a succession of

## 五、高频固定搭配
1. **make progress**
   释义：取得进展
   例句：We're making progress.
   替换：make headway / move forward / get ahead

2. **be responsible for**
   释义：对……负责
   例句：No Klingon was responsible for this attack.
   替换：be accountable for / be in charge of / be to blame for（贬义）

3. **it doesn’t matter**
   释义：没关系、不重要
   例句：Well, it doesn’t matter in the shuttle.
   替换：it’s irrelevant / it makes no difference / never mind

4. **on screen**
   释义：投屏、展示、上线
   例句：On screen, Lieutenant.
   替换：put it on display / show it on the monitor / bring it up on screen

# 二、中高级句式（Sentence Patterns）
1. **It has been my great honour to do…**
   释义：做……是我莫大的荣幸
   原文：It has been my great honour and joy to know you.
   适用：人物、经历、感谢类话题

2. **I’m afraid we didn’t accomplish a thing.**
   释义：恐怕我们一事无成（委婉表达失败/遗憾）
   原文：I’m afraid we didn't accomplish a thing.
   适用：描述计划、项目、努力无果

3. **Based on past history/experience, …**
   释义：根据以往经验……
   原文：Based on past history, I can’t accept that.
   适用：Part3 给出观点、论证

4. **Otherwise, …**
   释义：否则……（逻辑高分词）
   原文：Otherwise, the Federation will not exist much longer.
   适用：提出建议、警示后果

5. **It’s more than that.**
   释义：不止如此、没那么简单
   原文：Yes, but it’s more than that.
   适用：拓展观点，避免回答太浅

6. **There’s no such + 名词.**
   释义：不存在这样的……
   原文：There is no such ship in our fleet.
   适用：否定错误观点、澄清事实

# 一、Star Trek 专属·硬核科幻梗（只有 Trek 迷才懂，**雅思别直接用**）
这些是本IP独有的术语、设定，属于**科幻圈黑话**，日常口语/雅思不能直接照搬，但可以**理解背景**：
1. **Captain’s log / Stardate**
   原文：Captain's log, stardate 6031.2
   梗义：**舰长日志 / 星历**
   背景：星舰舰长每天必须记录的任务日志，**星历**是星际迷航独有的宇宙时间体系，不是地球日期。

2. **beam aboard / transporter**
   原文：awaiting the beaming aboard；having my atoms shooting through space
   梗义：**传送登舰 / 传送器**
   背景：星际迷航最经典黑科技——把人拆成分子传送到船上，很多角色都吐槽过“很吓人”。

3. **have the conn**
   原文：Scotty, you have the conn.
   梗义：**接管指挥权**
   背景：星舰**专属指挥术语**，= take command，只有舰长移交指挥权时说。

4. **Bridge**
   原文：Report to the bridge.
   梗义：**舰桥**（不是普通的桥）
   背景：星舰的**指挥中心**，所有核心操作都在这。

5. **Phasers / Disruptors**
   原文：Phasers targeted；His disrupters are armed.
   梗义：**相位枪 / 裂解炮**
   背景：**星际联邦**用相位枪，**克林贡人**用裂解炮，是两大阵营的标志性武器。

6. **Shields / Deflectors**
   原文：Shields up, sir；Secondary deflectors holding.
   梗义：**护盾 / 偏导仪**
   背景：星舰的防御系统，科幻片标配，但叫法是 Trek 专属。

7. **Cloak / Cloaking device**
   原文：if you were friendly, you wouldn’t have come in cloaked.
   梗义：**隐形 / 隐形装置**
   背景：克林贡、罗慕伦的专属黑科技，联邦早期没有。

8. **Warp engine / Impulse engine**
   原文：one warp engine, one impulse engine.
   梗义：**曲速引擎（超光速） / 脉冲引擎（常规航行）**
   背景：星际航行的两套动力系统，**曲速=超光速**是 Trek 核心设定。

9. **Sickbay**
   原文：Captain, report to Sickbay.
   梗义：**星舰医务室**（不是普通 hospital）。

10. **Klingon / Federation / Romulan**
    原文：Klingon vessel；Federation ambassadors；Romulan Star Empire
    梗义：**克林贡人 / 星际联邦 / 罗慕伦人**
    背景：星际迷航三大核心阵营，相当于“宇宙三国”。

# 三、通用科幻梗（不局限Star Trek，**雅思可以转化使用**）

1. **Space, the final frontier**
   原文：Space, the final frontier.
   可转化：宇宙是**最后的边疆**，用于描述**探索、科技、未来**话题。

2. **Explore strange new worlds / seek new life and civilizations**
   可转化：探索未知世界、寻找新文明，用于**科技发展、人类探索**类雅思话题。

3. **subspace Mayday**
   原文：send a subspace Mayday
   可转化：远距离求救信号，泛化为**call for help / emergency signal**。

4. **rapid aging disease**
   原文：a debilitating disease that is causing him to age rapidly
   可转化：**加速衰老**，用于**健康、科技、医学**话题。

# 雅思加分高级句式 + 影片原文例句
## 一、非谓语动词结构（雅思写作/口语**最高频加分**）
### 1. 现在分词作结果状语
**句式**：主句, doing...（自然而然的结果）
**加分点**：替代低级的 so/and
**原文例句**：
Charlie used the Guardian to kill Kirk's mother before he was born, **altering the entire timeline**.

### 2. 过去分词作定语/状语
**句式**：done..., 主句 / ...done...
**加分点**：简化定语从句，显语法功底
**原文例句**：
We have received a distress signal from an automated security beacon **orbiting a planet**.

### 3. 不定式作目的状语
**句式**：To do..., 主句 / ...to do...
**加分点**：替换低级的 in order to
**原文例句**：
They fight **to gain freedom** for everyone in the galaxy.

---

## 二、形式主语句（雅思大作文万能开头/论证）
### 句式：It + be + adj/n + that从句 / to do
**加分点**：避免头重脚轻，学术写作标配
**原文例句**：
**It is said** that if you move but one grain of sand you run the risk of altering history.

**同类高级句**：
**It is clear** that something is wrong within Starfleet.

## 三、比较级高级结构（雅思利弊/观点题必用）

**原文例句**：

### 2. the more..., the more... 越…越…
**句式**：The + 比较级, the + 比较级
**原文例句**：
**The longer** this situation continues, **the more** dangerous it becomes for the Federation.

## 四、让步状语从句（雅思观点/反驳段必用）
