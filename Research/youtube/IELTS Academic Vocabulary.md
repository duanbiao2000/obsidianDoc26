# 高分写作示例

普通表达：

> The study showed a big increase in temperature.

高级表达：

> The study **demonstrated** a **significant** increase in temperature.

---

普通表达：

> The government tried to reduce risk.

高级表达：

> The government **attempted to minimize** the risk.
