## 核心事实与信念

- 明确目标后，他发现「没有任何课程或老师能完全给到我想要的那种美式母语水平」，所以必须自己设计体系、自己负责结果。​
- 真正的转折点不是某个教材，而是他把英语变成生活环境：像母语一样「自然」「不需要刻意集中注意力」。​
- 单纯记忆、为了考试去学内容「根本留不住」，只有和现实生活、真实需求绑定的内容才会长期留下来。​
- 他自称没有语言天赋，现在的水平是「自己那套系统的产物」，证明方法本身有效，而不是靠天赋。​

## 关键类比与思维方式

- 目标设定：要有「自私但具体」的目标——例如「像美国人一样说英语」，这样在困难时目标本身会给你情绪上的驱动力。​
- 大脑模式：要让大脑把英语当作「正常背景噪音」而不是「一听到就竖起耳朵、非常紧张的信号」，像母语一样自然存在。​
- 场景想象：他常用「如果明天一醒来，世界变成全英文」的脑内模拟，倒推出自己每天真正需要用到的词汇和语法。​
- 自我对话 = 彩排：把对自己说英语当作「真实对话的彩排」，提前练习沟通动作，而不是等到真实对话才第一次尝试。​

## 可执行步骤（Action Steps）

## 1. 目标与环境

- 设定一个具体、对你本人「情绪上很重要」的英语目标（例如能无压力地在某个国家生活、工作在某个岗位）。​
- 彻底把所有设备和常用网站切换成英语：手机、电脑、系统语言、应用和网站界面等，强迫自己在真实操作中读懂英语。​
- 把你的娱乐和爱好迁移到英语：电影、剧集、YouTube、博客、社交媒体等都用英语版本，同时选择你本来就喜欢的主题，让自己在「开心、放松」状态下输入英语。​
- 通过这些输入打造「人为的全英文环境」，直到听到英语时不再紧张，而是觉得「很正常」。​

## 2. 围绕真实需求学习

- 分析你现实生活中的对话需求：你平时常聊什么话题、别人经常向你请教什么、你的专业或兴趣领域是什么。​
- 根据这些真实话题决定接下来要学的单词、表达和语法，而不是随便从教材顺序走。​
- 对每一个要学的点，走完他的「三步系统」：
    1）先弄懂概念本身（语法点或词义）；
    2）在练习或习题中使用它（做题、造句、填空等）；
    3）用它来表达你自己的真实想法和经历（写段落、录音、自言自语）。​

## 3. 主动输出与练习

- 在网络上加入你真正感兴趣的英语社区：Facebook 群组、Twitter、YouTube 评论、博客和粉丝页等，目标是「讨论你关心的话题」，而不是「机械练习英语」。​
- 经常在这些地方发言，把新学的单词、表达和语法刻意用出来，让自己「被听见」，训练真实交流中的反应能力。​
- 大量而频繁地用英语自言自语：一个人时用英语描述你在做什么、在想什么，或复盘一天发生的事，把这个当作「对话动作的高频训练」。​

## 4. 语言形式 + 文化输入

- 有意识地研究目标国家的文化：看视频、电影、节目时，观察人们怎么开玩笑、用什么参考、习惯用哪些习语和短语动词。​
- 模仿他们真实讲话的方式（节奏、词汇、常见句型），而不只停留在课本里的「教科书式表达」。​

如果把这些步骤都执行到位，你的系统就会和他说的一样：先通过环境和情绪让大脑放下防备，再用真实需求驱动的三步循环，把输入不断转化为你自己的输出。​

## 1. 真正的产品，是「系统」而不是「天赋」

** latent insight：**
他在反复强调「没有天赋」「是系统的产物」，隐含意思是：可复制的是一套思维和环境配置，而不是某几本教材或老师。

- 原话示例：
    「I don't have any talent for languages. My current level is the result of the system I built, not some natural gift.」​

** second‑order consequence：**
这意味着：如果你还在追「神课」「神老师」，你其实把注意力放在不可复制变量上，反而延迟了真正有效的系统建设（环境、习惯、反馈回路）。​

## 2. 把英语变成『背景噪音』，是在重写大脑的威胁模型

** latent insight：**
他不是单纯说「多听」，而是要让英语成为「背景」而不是「前景」，也就是大脑从「警报模式」切换到「默认安全模式」。这本质上是改写你对英语的情绪标签。​

- 原话示例：
    「You need to make English like background noise, something your brain doesn't panic about, the same way it treats your native language.」​

** second‑order consequence：**
如果你不先解决这个「威胁感」，再多输入都被大脑当作压力事件处理，记忆效率会持续偏低；相反，一旦英语被标记为「安全又有奖励」，你每天自然会去接触，长期输入变成自驱动而不是意志力硬抗。​

## 3. 目标必须「自私且带情绪」，否则长期行为不会收敛

** latent insight：**
他不是让你设「模糊的大目标」，而是强调目标要「跟你本人情绪强绑定」——例如「在美国生活得像当地人」。这会直接影响你每天会不会自动为目标让路。​

- 原话示例：
    「Your goal has to be almost selfish and very specific. Mine was: I want to sound like an American and live there like a local.」​

** second‑order consequence：**
如果目标只是「英语变好一点」「考个高分」，那在与其他欲望（刷短视频、打游戏）竞争时会长期输掉，因为这些目标没有足够情绪强度来驱动你的时间分配。结果是：计划设计得再好，行为层面还是会崩盘。​

---

## 4. 「三步系统」的核心，是强制把知识从输入端推到输出端

** latent insight：**
他那套「先理解 → 再练习 → 再用来表达自己的真实想法」，是一个有方向性的通道：每一点新知识，最终都要经过「个人化输出」这道关卡，否则等于没进系统。​

- 原话示例：
    「For anything I wanted to learn, I had three steps: understand it, practice it, then use it to express my own ideas and experiences. If I skipped that last part, it didn't stick.」​

** second‑order consequence：**
这意味着刷题、背单词如果没有落到「我用它说我自己」，就不会长期驻留，你会不断产生一种错觉：学了很多，但一开口就清空。长期下去，会直接打击自我效能感，让人觉得「我是不是学不进去」。​

## 5. 把娱乐和爱好迁移到英语，是在重构「多巴胺来源」

** latent insight：**
他不是简单说「看美剧学英语」，而是把**所有**原本就能给你快乐的活动迁移到英语环境，这等于把多巴胺奖励和英语紧密绑定。​

- 原话示例：
    「Move your hobbies into English. The things that already make you happy should now mostly happen in English, so your brain feels rewarded every time it meets the language.」​

** second‑order consequence：**
当「快乐」持续和英语共同出现时，大脑会自动提升对英语相关线索的注意力和记忆优先级，你不用再依靠意志力维持学习；反之，如果英语只在「痛苦场景」（考试、作业）出现，你会本能地逃避它。​

---

## 6. 自言自语和线上互动，是在预支「未来对话」

** latent insight：**
他把自言自语、在网络上发言，看作是「真实对话的预演」，本质是在预支未来情境，把「第一次犯错」放在低成本环境中消化掉。​

- 原话示例：
    「Talking to yourself in English is just rehearsal for real conversations. Same with commenting online. You are making your first mistakes in a place where it is cheap to fail.」​

** second‑order consequence：**
如果你大量做这种「预演」，真正遇到真人时，大脑会识别为「熟悉脚本」，焦虑和卡壳大幅下降；如果完全不预演，第一次输出都发生在高压力场景，你会形成「我一说就卡」的负面信念，之后越来越不敢开口。​

---

## 7. 围绕真实需求学，是在给大脑建立「索引树」

** latent insight：**
他让你围绕自己真实生活和专业来挑素材，而不是听课本的顺序，这等于先有「语义网络」，再往上挂词汇和语法。​

- 原话示例：
    「Start from what you actually talk about in your life and your field. Then learn the vocabulary and grammar you need for those conversations, not the other way round.」​

** second‑order consequence：**
这样学的结果是：每个新词新结构都能立刻挂在一个具体的「节点」上（某个场景、某个朋友、某次对话），检索效率会越来越高；如果你从抽象列表开始，记忆是平面的，没有索引，很快就互相覆盖和遗忘。​

## 三步“流利系统”

视频把流利拆成三个步骤：[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​

1. “Step one is options… grammar, vocabulary… You obviously can't speak without options.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
2. “The second step is being able to access those options in real time… When you're relaxed, your English is there… under pressure… the words don't disappear, they are just locked away.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
3. “Step three… your ability to make a choice and stick with it even under pressure… Fluency is not correctness, it's commitment.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​

理解了“流利 = 有选项 + 能在压力下取用 + 迅速做决定并坚持”，你就自然会把练习重心从“多背一点”转到“在压力下快速做不完美的决定并持续说下去”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​

## 对你学习方式的含义

- 如果你已经 B1 左右，“most of you probably already have enough options.” 真正缺的是第二、第三步的训练，而不是再多几百个单词。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
- “Every time you allow yourself to stop mid‑sentence, correct yourself, go back, restart, you are training hesitation.” 也就是说，你现在的很多习惯其实在“刻意练犹豫”，而不是练流利。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​

一个简单的例子：以后做口语练习，可以给自己设规则：一句话说出口就不回头改，只能往前补充或澄清，用这种方式训练“速度和连续性优先于完美”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​

他讲的核心是“流利 = 在压力下做决定并坚持”，从这里往下推，会有一堆他没明说但必然成立的东西。

## 1. 你的“教材型进步”早就严重过量

- 他说：大多数 B1 学习者已经有足够的选项（词汇语法），问题不在知识。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    “Quite honestly, if you are a B1 level learner, you have enough options.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    
- 隐含结论：
    对很多中级以上学习者来说，再刷一轮单词书、再多一门语法课，边际收益已经极低，甚至是在浪费“可以用来训练决策的时间”。
    
- 换句话说，你看到“我还不够好”的痛感，真实来源是“不会用已有东西”，而不是“真的缺材料”。

## 2. 纠错型课堂和考试导向训练，会直接“反向训练”流利

- 他说：每次停下来、回头重说，你在训练犹豫；而犹豫杀死流利比错误快得多。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    “Every time you allow yourself to stop mid-sentence, correct yourself, go back, restart, you are training hesitation. And hesitation kills fluency much faster than mistakes ever will.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    
- 隐含结论：
    那种老师随时打断你、逼你当场改到“最完美”的课堂模式，其实在系统性地破坏你在压力下“做决定并往前说”的能力。
    
- 这也意味着：如果你常年泡这种环境，再怎么“学了很多”，一到真实对话还是会卡——因为你的大脑被条件反射成：一感到不确定就立刻刹车。

## 3. “纠结最佳表达”其实是一种自恋式注意力错位

- 他描述非流利说话者：不停地问“这是最好的说法吗？最合适的吗？”，然后开始“挖最好的选项”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    “Is this the most appropriate way? Is this the best way? And then you just start digging the best way.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    
- 同时他说：流利者只选第一个“足够可以”的选项，目标是成功交流，而不是炫耀自己庞大的知识库。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    “Fluent speakers don't search for the best option. They choose the most acceptable option that comes first… because the goal is to have a successful conversation, not to impress someone with your massive English knowledge base.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    
- 隐含结论：
    每当你卡住在“有没有更高级、更地道、更完美的说法”，注意力已经从“对方 / 对话”转移到“自我形象管理”，这是一种极隐蔽的自恋型执念。
    真正流利的人其实“自我形象阈值更低”：我只要说得过去就行，重点在交流。

## 4. 口语焦虑本质上是“决策焦虑”，不是“英语焦虑”

- 他开头直接讲：你不是因为英语差才 freeze，是因为不会在压力下做决定。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    “You don't freeze because your English is bad. You freeze because you don't know how to decide under pressure.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    
- 隐含结论：
    你在英语里表现出来的 panic / blank，不是语言专属问题，而是你人格层面普遍存在的“害怕做不完美决定”的模式在英语里被放大。
    
- 他甚至半开玩笑说：如果你现实中有“commitment issues”，可以从这里练起。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    “If you have commitment issues in real life… this is a good way to start addressing this problem through this.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    
- 所以：练“英语流利”其实是一种“安全、低成本的决策力训练场”，能反向改善你其他领域的拖延和选择困难。

## 5. 大量“输入但不输出”的学习路径，本质是训练一种“只认不说”的大脑结构

- 他区分了被动识别和主动提取，强调要把学到的内容从“能认出来”变成“能在正确时刻想得起来”，称为 active style learning。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    “Make sure you really activate it… so that you don't just recognize it when you read it or hear it somewhere. You can actually think of it when the time is right. That's what we call active style learning.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    
- 隐含结论：
    仅仅“多看、多听、不说”的沉浸式输入，如果不刻意包含“主动提取”的环节，会系统性地把你的神经网络塑造成“只能理解，不能启动生产”的模式。
    
- 换句话说：“我能看懂一切但张口就废”不是意外，而是完全符合你长期操作方式的必然结果。

---

## 6. 你不只是“没练够口语”，而是从一开始就选错了训练目标

- 他明确说：如果你已经会但在压力下用不出来，你不需要更多规则，而是需要“正确的说话系统”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    “If you already know English but can't use it under pressure, you don't need more rules. You need the right speaking system.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    
- 隐含结论：
    很多学习者以为自己是在“练口语”，但实际的训练对象仍然是“知识本身”（新词、新句型），而不是“在时间压力和社交压力下保持连续输出”。
    
- 所以：真正意义上的口语训练，衡量标准不应该是“今天学了多少新表达”，而是“今天在多大压力下，保持了多少秒不被打断的连续输出”。

---

## 7. “完美主义 + 高级表达执念”会让你永远停在 B2 围城

- 他提到中高级阶段“卡住”“进步不再像以前那样”，并给出从 intermediate 到 advanced 的课程作为“出路”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
- 结合他的框架：
    - B1 前：主要缺“选项”（词汇语法） → 多学有用
    - B1 之后：选项已经够多，但决策和提取没有同步升级 → 看起来“永远在 B2 打转”
- 隐含结论：
    如果你在 B2 附近一直刷更高级的词汇语法，却没有刻意训练“牺牲完美以换取速度和连续性”，你会形成一种非常舒服但危险的 zone：理论超强，现实对话像 B1。
    直到你主动放弃“展示自己英语有多高级”的冲动，你才可能真的跨进 C1 的使用能力。

---

## 8. 流利训练本质是“速度训练”，不是“语言训练”

- 他自己说：把它看成训练 speed，而不是 training language。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    “So, think of it as more like training speed rather than training language.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SB9jW56OT2Y)]​
    
- 隐含结论：
    任何让你不断停顿、回放、反复推敲的练习（例如一句话打磨 3 分钟），从“流利”的角度看都是在逆向训练。

## Core facts

- Reading is one of the most powerful and underrated ways to improve English and build natural fluency.[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ecGj3vKE4C0)]​
    
- Reading gives you full natural sentences, so you learn meanings, usage, expressions, grammar, rhythm, and flow together in context instead of isolated words or rules.[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ecGj3vKE4C0)]​
    
- Seeing the same word several times in different sentences helps your brain remember it faster than drilling flashcards.[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ecGj3vKE4C0)]​
    
- The main _real_ problem is that reading in another language is tiring: stopping every few seconds to check words makes people quit, even though reading itself works.[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ecGj3vKE4C0)]​
    
- Using a tool that lets you tap a word to see meaning, hear pronunciation, and save it in one place removes friction and keeps you focused on reading.[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ecGj3vKE4C0)]​
    
- As vocabulary grows, learners can move from supported digital reading to reading real books on their own.[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ecGj3vKE4C0)]​
    
- Consistency (reading a little every day) is more important than intensity (reading a huge amount rarely) for building fluency.[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ecGj3vKE4C0)]​
    
- The app he recommends for this workflow is LingQ, created by Steve Kaufmann, designed for immersive reading with instant lookup and review.[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ecGj3vKE4C0)]​
    

## Key analogies / framing

- Start with **short** content you actually enjoy: blog posts, tweets, song lyrics, Reddit threads, or other brief texts.[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ecGj3vKE4C0)]​

## 2. 关键类比与示例（附原话）

## 2.1 “只听关键词”示例

他用一句话演示“只保留重点词也能理解大意”：  
完整句子：  
“My friends invited me to go to the beach but I don't want to go.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​

挑出的焦点词：  
“friends invited beach but don't want.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​

作者原话：

- “focus words are basically the most important word in your sentence…”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
    
- “let's say I get rid of everything else… this is what you get: friends invited beach but don't want… even just with that you can understand what's going on.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
    

隐含类比：听英语时不要试图“看清每一片树叶”，而是先看清“这片森林的几棵大树”（焦点词）。youtube+1

## 2.2 “把用力音变成很省力的‘呃’”

他用 /tuː/ → /tə/ 说明弱读本质是“省力、制造对比”：

- “to reduce in English means to make something smaller.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
    
- “for example this word right here ‘to’… this is a very high effort sound ‘oo’… why don't we just replace ‘oo’ with the sound ‘uh’ and instead of saying ‘to’ why don't we just say ‘tə’.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
    
- “invited me to go, we're going to say invited me tə go.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
    

## 2.3 “把句子说成一个单词”

他把整句连起来，说明母语者追求的是“一次振动”：

- “we always want to say one sentence like it's one word as much as possible, single vibration.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
    
- 示例发音：  
    “my friends invited me to go to the beach but I don't want to go.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​

## 3. 三个“承重”技巧 + 可执行步骤（附原话）

## 3.1 技巧一：注意力/心态转变（mindset / focus shift）

核心问题：一听不懂就慌，结果越听越少。

- “we tend to panic and freak out and get a little nervous and try to think back to what was said 2 seconds ago and then you keep missing everything that's being said currently.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
    

关键做法：

1. 只关注你听得懂的词。
    
    - “the number one thing you want to think about is focusing only on things that you understand. Everything else doesn't exist.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
        
2. 用已知词来“补全故事”。
    
    - “you'll be able to sort of build and recreate the whole picture… based only on the words that you do know.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
        
3. 像母语者那样，只抓大意。
    
    - “this is typically how most native speakers listen to one another and recognize what the overall idea of the statement is.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
        

可执行练习：

- 每次听视频时，强迫自己写下 3–5 个你确定听到的“重点词”，用这些词用母语或英语写一句“可能的意思”。youtube+1
    

---

## 3.2 技巧二：弱读（reductions）

核心定义：

- “reductions in English are… making it more effortless… simplifying sounds that take effort… we need to create even more of a contrast when we speak.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
    

关键现象（需要听出来，而不是按拼写“硬听”）：

1. to → tə：
    
    - “instead of saying ‘to’ why don't we just say ‘tə’… invited me tə go.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
        
2. 双元音变成一个很短的元音（my / I）：
    
    - “this word right here… ‘my’… we're going to replace it with the sound ‘ə’… my friends → mə friends.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
        
    - “‘I’ is two vowels in one… we're just going to replace it with one… but I don't want to go → but ə don't wanna go.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
        
3. want to → wanna：
    
    - “we basically just say ‘wə tə’… we just say ‘wanna’, it's much easier, ‘don't wanna’.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
        

可执行练习：

- 选一两句你常说的话（比如 “I want to do it”），刻意用“弱读版”重复：  
    “I wanna do it” → “ə wanna do ɪt”，录下来对比清楚版和弱读版。youtube+1
    

---

## 3.3 技巧三：连读（linking）

总原则：把一句话尽量说成“一次振动”的连续气流。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​

三种主要连读：

1. 辅音 + 元音：把前一个词的尾辅音“挂”到下个词开头
    
    - “if your first word ends with a consonant sound and your second word starts with a vowel… treat this ending consonant sound as the first sound of the next word.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
        
    - “friends invited → friend-zinvited.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
        
2. 元音 + 辅音：不切断元音，直接滑到辅音
    
    - “from vowel to consonant… you just don't stop the vowel, don't create a space, move on straight to the consonant… me to go, to the beach.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
        
3. “爆破音 + 辅音”（stop consonant linking）：只做“准备”，不真正把音爆出来
    
    - “what a stop consonant is… you set it up first… then you blow out air and vocal vibrations… if you don't release your tongue nothing's going to happen.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
        
    - “what you do here is just you do not release and you move on straight to the next consonant… invited me → invite(d)m e, don't wanna.”youtube+1
        

综合到那句示例：

- “my friends invited me to go to the beach but I don't want to go.”（中间几乎不断气，只在 but 前后刻意停一下。）[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​
    

可执行练习：

- 拿这句示例，先逐词读，再按他示范的连读方式读，目标是整句只停两次：在 beach 和 but 前后稍微停顿。录音对比自己的断点位置。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7mCp-NjXi5c)]​

## 2）整份词汇表的系统练习

用途：你已经有一份本周词汇表，不想干背，而是想用练习来“drill these words”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

必要提示词（作者原话骨架）：

> “I'm trying to learn this vocabulary in English: **[insert your list]**. Please come up with some practice activities to help me learn them much quicker. You can play around with multiple-choice tests, fill in the blank tasks, etc. I really want to drill these words and get very confident and comfortable using them.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

负载要点：

- 先准备好自己的单词列表。
    
- 让 AI 生成多种练习：选择题、填空等。
    
- 明确目标：要“drill these words”并“get very confident and comfortable using them”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

## 3）围绕特定主题“找词”

用途：你还没有词表，只是想围绕某个主题（餐厅、机场、关系等）找到“重要的常用词和表达”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

必要提示词（作者原话骨架）：

> “I'm interested in learning important and commonly used vocabulary related to **[topic]**. Please give me a list of words and useful expressions that I can memorize and start practicing immediately. Please keep it under 10.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

负载要点：

- 主题由你填：relationships, family, airport, restaurant 等。
    
- 明确限制：keep it under 10，避免一次输出太多而无法消化。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

## 文本对话练习：两个核心场景

## 4）自由聊天 + 即时纠错

用途：和 AI “闲聊”练习英语，同时要求对方立刻指出并更正你的错误，并告诉你应该学什么。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

必要提示词（作者原话）：

> “I want to have a conversation with you in English. If I make any mistakes, please identify them and correct them right away. If it's a grammar mistake, then suggest what I should study to improve my skills. Please write the corrections in English. Let's begin.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

负载要点：

- 明确要求“identify them and correct them right away”。
    
- 要求在语法错时给出“what I should study”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​
    

---

## 5）情境模拟对话（chat style）

用途：模拟某个具体情境（餐厅点餐、机场值机、酒店 check-in、会议等），在“真实场景”下练习对话并即时纠错。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

必要提示词（作者原话骨架）：

> “I want to practice a conversation in English in a specific situation. Let's play out the following scenario: **[explain the situation in detail]**. Be as specific as you can and insert as many important details as you can so that you can take this information and use it to create this entire scene so that it's natural and not super weird. If I make a mistake, feel free to pause the conversation to address it right away. Explain what I did wrong and provide a correction. Let's begin.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

负载要点：

- 说明“具体情境 + 重要细节”，让对话自然、不奇怪。
    
- 授权 AI “pause the conversation” 来讲解错误。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​
    

示例中的关键纠错：

- 学生说：“My friend want to had seafood pasta.”
    
- AI 纠正：“My friend wants to have the seafood pasta.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​
    

这段例子展示了：AI 会在对话中即时指出错误并给出更自然的版本。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

## 语音模式：最关键的用法

## 6）语音模式情境对话（voice simulation）

用途：打开语音，像现实中“打电话/对话”那样练习，特别适用于餐厅、电话、工作场景等口语情境。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

步骤＋必要提示词（作者原话骨架）：

1. 打开语音模式：  
    “First you will activate voice mode…”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​
    
2. 提示词：
    

> “Hey ChatGPT, I want to practice a conversation in English in a specific situation. Let's play out the following scenario: **[explain the situation]**. If I make a mistake, feel free to pause the conversation to address it right away. Explain what I did wrong and provide a correction.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

负载要点：

- 和文本情境相同，但改为“语音交互”，更接近真实口语。
    
- 目标：测试自己在具体场景中是否能“navigate these situations successfully”，同时让 AI 帮你“assess and evaluate what you need”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​
    

作者原话（关于效果）：

> “It's a really cool way to test yourself in a very specific situation… It can really help you gain more confidence and really practice those things and most importantly assess and evaluate what you need to be able to navigate these situations successfully.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​

## 作者对学习方式的核心论点

- 只“坐着背单词”是“not going to work”，你需要在“action”中使用它们。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​
    
- 这些提示词的目的，是让你“actually practice and learn and be productive, but do it in a really interesting, fun way.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​
    
- 把词汇练好后，可以“带到这里”来和 AI 反复练习，形成完整闭环（学词 → 做练习 → 情境对话）。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​
    

作者原话（节选）：

> “You want to use them in action. And ChatGPT or any other AI language model can help you with that.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​  
> “All you got to do is just use these prompts that I gave you and have fun and go crazy and actually practice and learn and be productive, but do it in a really interesting, fun way.”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EbDU3YbSe4)]​
