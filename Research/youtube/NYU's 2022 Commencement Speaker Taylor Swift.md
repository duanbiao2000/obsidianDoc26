## 致谢和场合用语

1. **I would like to say a huge thank you to …**
    含义：我想向……表达衷心感谢（正式又自然）。  
    示例：I’d like to say a huge thank you to everyone who helped on this project.

2. **I feel so proud to share this day with …**
    含义：很荣幸能和……共同见证今天（典型毕业/颁奖场合）。  
    示例：I feel so proud to share this day with my family and my colleagues.

3. **Let me say to you now, welcome to New York, it’s been waiting for you.**
    含义：让我对你们说一句：欢迎来到纽约，它一直在等你。（欢迎 + 仪式感）  
    示例：Let me say to you now, welcome to our team, we’ve been waiting for you.

4. **I know that words are supposed to be my thing, but I will never be able to find the words to thank …**
    含义：我明明是“玩文字的”，却还是找不到合适的话来感谢……（自谦+感谢）。  
    示例：I will never be able to find the words to thank my parents for their support.

## 人生建议 / 哲理类金句

1. **Life can be heavy, especially if you try to carry it all at once.**
    含义：生活会很沉重，尤其当你想一次扛起所有东西时。  
    示例：Life can be heavy, especially if you try to carry it all at once, so it’s okay to ask for help.

2. **Part of growing up is about catch and release.**
    含义：成长的一部分，就是学会抓住和放下。  
    示例：Part of growing up is about catch and release: keep what helps you, let go of what hurts.

3. **Decide what is yours to hold and let the rest go.**
    含义：决定哪些是你该抓住的，其余的就放手。  
    示例：You have to decide what is yours to hold and let the rest go, especially when you’re overwhelmed.

4. **You get to pick what your life has time and room for.**
    含义：你可以自己决定，生活有时间和空间容纳什么。  
    示例：You get to pick what your life has time and room for, so don’t fill it only with work.

5. **Losing things doesn’t just mean losing.**
    含义：失去某些东西，并不只是失去。  
    示例：Remember that losing things doesn’t just mean losing; sometimes it clears space for something better.

6. **A lot of the time when we lose things, we gain things too.**
    含义：很多时候，当我们失去一些东西，也在得到一些东西。  
    示例：When a plan fails, a lot of the time when we lose things, we gain things too, like new skills or friends.

## 态度和性格表达

1. **Be discerning.**
    含义：要有辨别力、要懂得筛选。  
    示例：When you read things online, be discerning about which voices you trust.

2. **Learn to live alongside cringe.**
    含义：学会和“尴尬/中二/自我嫌弃感”共存。  
    示例：If you want to improve your speaking, you have to learn to live alongside cringe.

3. **Never be ashamed of trying. Effortlessness is a myth.**
    含义：永远不要为努力而羞耻，“毫不费力”是个神话。  
    示例：Never be ashamed of trying; effortlessness is a myth in any real achievement.

4. **I’m a big advocate for not hiding your enthusiasm for things.**
    含义：我非常支持不要隐藏你对事物的热情。  
    示例：I’m a big advocate for not hiding your enthusiasm for things you love, even if others think it’s nerdy.

## 决策与选择

1. **It’s totally up to you.**
    含义：完全取决于你。（既可以是中性，也可以是强调责任在你）  
    示例：You can stay or you can leave; it’s totally up to you.
