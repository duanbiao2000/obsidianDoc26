除了 "weak words"（弱语）之外，雅思（IELTS）中区分 **Band 7** 与 **Band 9** 的另一个关键因素是**语言的多样性和精确性（Lexical Resource）**。以下是一些需要特别关注的方面，并附有实例：

---

### 1. **词汇的精确性 vs. 模糊性 (Precision vs. Vagueness)**

**7分考生**倾向于使用模糊、笼统的词汇，而**9分考生**则使用更精确、具体的词汇。

| 弱语 (Weak) | 强语 (Strong) |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Many people" | "A significant proportion of the population" |
| "A lot of problems" | "Numerous systemic issues" |
| "Very good" | "Exemplary, outstanding, superior" |

**实例对比**：

- **Weak**: "Technology has changed **many things** in our lives."
- **Strong**: "Technology has revolutionized **numerous aspects** of modern life, from communication to healthcare."

---

### 2. **词汇的多样性 vs. 重复性 (Diversity vs. Repetition)**

**7分考生**常用有限的词汇，而**9分考生**则能灵活运用同义词、近义词和不同的句式。

| 弱语 (Weak) | 强语 (Strong) |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Important" (多次重复) | "Crucial, pivotal, significant, vital" |
| "Bad" (多次重复) | "Detrimental, adverse, harmful, deleterious" |
| "Effect" (多次重复) | "Impact, consequence, influence, outcome" |

**实例对比**：

- **Weak**: "Climate change has **important effects** on weather patterns. It has **important effects** on agriculture. It has **important effects** on ecosystems."
- **Strong**: "Climate change has **pivotal consequences** for weather patterns. It exerts a **significant impact** on agricultural productivity. It poses a **serious threat** to ecosystems."

---

### 3. **学术词汇的使用 (Use of Academic Vocabulary)**

**7分考生**倾向于使用口语化、非正式的词汇，而**9分考生**则使用更多学术词汇。

| 弱语 (Weak) | 强语 (Strong) |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Big" | "Substantial, considerable, immense, vast" |
| "Small" | "Minimal, negligible, insignificant, trivial" |
| "Good" | "Advantageous, beneficial, conducive, advantageous" |

**实例对比**：

- **Weak**: "This policy has **big effects** on the economy."
- **Strong**: "This policy has **substantial effects** on the economy, particularly on employment rates."

---

### 4. **词汇的搭配准确性 (Collocation Accuracy)**

**7分考生**常出现不自然的词汇搭配，而**9分考生**则使用符合习惯搭配的词汇。

| 弱语 (Weak) | 强语 (Strong) |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Economic growth" (正确) | "Economic growth" (正确) |
| "Economic fast" (错误) | "Rapid economic growth" (正确) |
| "Technology advance" (错误) | "Technological advancement" (正确) |

**实例对比**：

- **Weak**: "We need technology to advance economy."
- **Strong**: "We need technological advancement to drive economic growth."

---

### 5. **词汇的复杂性 (Complexity of Vocabulary)**

**7分考生**倾向于使用简单的词汇，而**9分考生**则使用更复杂的词汇结构。

| 弱语 (Weak) | 强语 (Strong) |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Technology is good." | "The proliferation of technology has precipitated a paradigm shift in societal norms." |
| "People are happy." | "The demographic exhibits a marked increase in life satisfaction indices." |

**实例对比**：

- **Weak**: "Technology makes life easier."
- **Strong**: "The advent of digital technology has **facilitated unprecedented levels of connectivity and efficiency**."

---

### 6. **词汇的语境相关性 (Contextual Relevance)**

**7分考生**常使用与语境不相关的词汇，而**9分考生**则使用更贴切的词汇。

| 弱语 (Weak) | 强语 (Strong) |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Technology is important in education." | "The integration of digital tools has **transformed pedagogical approaches**, fostering a more interactive and personalized learning environment." |
| "Economy is good." | "The **robust economic growth** in the last decade has been **attributable to** strategic policy implementations and global trade expansions." |

---

### 总结

在雅思中，**词汇的多样性、精确性和语境相关性**是区分 **Band 7** 与 **Band 9** 的关键。**9分考生**不仅使用更丰富的词汇，而且能根据语境灵活运用，使表达更精确、更有说服力。通过刻意练习和积累，考生可以显著提升自己的词汇能力，从而在雅思考试中取得更高的分数。

**底层需求：** 系统性理解7→9的全维度差距，而不只是词汇层面的修补。

## 7分 vs 9分：六大核心差距

---

### 一、论点结构：陈述 vs 论证

7分考生**告诉你结论**，9分考生**建立论证链**。

**7分：**

> "Social media is bad for mental health. Many young people use it too much and feel depressed."

结构：观点 → 重复观点（换了词）

**9分：**

> "Heavy social media use correlates with depression not because of content itself, but because it displaces sleep and face-to-face interaction — the two variables most predictive of adolescent wellbeing."

结构：观点 → **机制** → 为什么这个机制重要

**公式：** Claim → **Because** → **Which means** → Therefore

---

### 二、例子质量：举例 vs 论证

7分的例子是**装饰**，9分的例子是**武器**。

**7分：**

> "For example, in China, the government controls the internet."

给了例子，但它证明了什么？对论点有什么推进？完全不清楚。

**9分：**

> "China's Great Firewall illustrates how information control shapes not just behaviour but cognition — a generation raised without access to contradictory sources develops fundamentally different risk-assessment patterns."

例子 → **说明什么机制** → **延伸到更大的论点**

**规则：** 每个例子后问：So what does this prove about my argument? 这句话必须说出来。

---

### 三、让步处理：忽视 vs 吸收

7分考生**绕开反驳**，9分考生**吸收反驳后立场更强**。

**7分：**

> "Some people think nuclear energy is dangerous, but I disagree because it is clean."

让步是假的——完全没有处理对方的论点。

**9分：**

> "Nuclear energy carries real catastrophic risk — Chernobyl and Fukushima are not statistical anomalies but systemic failures. Yet the relevant comparison is not 'nuclear vs. safe' but 'nuclear vs. coal', which kills an estimated 800,000 people annually through air pollution. On that metric, the calculus shifts decisively."

结构：**承认对方最强的论点** → 重新定义比较框架 → 己方立场在新框架下胜出

---

### 四、句子节奏：单一 vs 对比张力

9分的句子有**内部张力**，读起来有推进感。

**7分（节奏平）：**

> "Technology has many benefits. It helps people communicate. It also makes work easier. However, there are some disadvantages."

四句话，信息密度极低，考官读完不记得任何东西。

**9分（有张力）：**

> "Technology compresses distance but amplifies isolation — we have never been more connected to strangers, or more estranged from neighbours."

技巧：

- **对比结构** X but Y / not X but Y
- **悖论陈述** 先给反直觉的observation
- **短句收尾** 长句铺垫 + 短句击中

---

### 五、话题句控制力：模糊 vs 精准预告

7分的段落第一句**没有承诺**，读者不知道这段要证明什么。

**7分话题句：**

> "There are many reasons why education is important."

这句话是空的——"many reasons"等于没说。

**9分话题句：**

> "The most underestimated function of education is not knowledge transfer, but the calibration of uncertainty tolerance."

立刻**建立具体论点** + **给出反常识角度** + 读者知道接下来要看什么论证。

**测试方法：** 只读每段第一句——能否还原整篇文章的论证结构？9分可以，7分不能。

---

### 六、口语：信息填充 vs 思维过程可见

口语Part 3的本质不是考知识，是考**在压力下实时建构论点的能力**。

**7分（用filler买时间，但思维不可见）：**

> "Hmm, that's a good question… I think… um… maybe… people should… you know… care more about the environment."

**9分（用语言让思维过程可见）：**

> "That's a tension worth unpacking — on one hand X, but the more I think about it, the stronger case is actually Y, because…"

| 7分 filler              | 9分 替换                                               |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Um / Uh / You know     | "The way I'd frame this is…"                        |
| That's a good question | 直接进入，或 "There are two ways to approach this"        |
| I'm not sure but maybe | "The evidence points in different directions, but…" |

---

### 总结：7→9的本质跃迁

|维度|7分|9分|
|---|---|---|
|论点|告诉结论|建立机制|
|例子|装饰|武器|
|让步|回避|吸收并反超|
|句子|平铺|内部张力|
|段落|话题句模糊|精准预告论证|
|口语|填充时间|思维过程可见|

**核心洞察：** 7分证明你**会英语**，9分证明你**用英语思考**。差距不在词汇表，在论证密度。

除了“弱语”之外，雅思 **7分 → 9分** 的跨越本质上是 **思维精度、语言控制力与学术自然度** 的质变。7分是“达标且清晰”，9分是“精准、灵活、无痕迹”。以下按雅思官方评分维度，拆解必须关注的核心差异，并附真实语境对比：

---

### 🔑 核心逻辑对照

| 维度 | Band 7 特征 | Band 9 特征 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Task Response** | 覆盖题目要求，立场清晰，论点有支撑，但可能偏模板化或泛化 | 全面回应隐含要求，展现批判性思维，论点有层次/让步/延伸，例证具体 |
| **Coherence & Cohesion** | 逻辑连贯，正确使用连接词，段落分明，但有时显机械 | 逻辑推进如呼吸般自然，衔接靠语义/指代/句式呼应，几乎不用显性连接词 |
| **Lexical Resource** | 使用非常见词汇，偶有搭配不当或语体不一致 | 搭配地道、词义精准、语体完全匹配语境，零“大词堆砌” |
| **Grammar** | 多样复杂句，基本准确，偶有僵硬或微小错误 | 结构服务于意义，灵活使用倒装/分词/名词化/虚拟等，完全准确 |
| **Speaking (FC & P)** | 能持续输出，有少量自我修正/填充词，语调清晰但较平 | 极少犹豫，话语标记自然，连读/弱读/重音精准传递态度与逻辑重心 |

---

### 📝 1. Task Response：观点深度 vs 表面覆盖

**7分**：回答题目，给出立场+例子，但停留在“常识层面”。
**9分**：挖掘题目隐含维度，展现权衡、边界条件或长期影响。

| 题目 | Band 7 | Band 9 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| *Should universities focus on employability or academic research?* | Universities should teach practical skills because students need jobs. Research is also important but less urgent. For example, engineering graduates get hired faster. | While employability ensures immediate socioeconomic integration, prioritizing it at the expense of fundamental research risks stagnating long-term innovation. A symbiotic model, where applied curricula are informed by cutting-edge scholarship, ultimately yields both workforce readiness and scientific advancement. |

💡 **提分关键**：不要只答“是什么”，要答“在什么条件下成立/不成立”“对谁影响最大”“长期 vs 短期权衡”。

---

### 🔗 2. Coherence & Cohesion：隐形逻辑链 vs 机械连接词

**7分**：依赖 `Firstly, However, In conclusion, On the other hand`，段落像拼图。
**9分**：靠**代词指代、同义替换、句式呼应、逻辑递进**自然衔接，连接词仅用于强调转折/让步。

| 段落推进 | Band 7 | Band 9 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| 讨论远程办公 | Firstly, remote work saves commuting time. Secondly, it reduces office costs. However, it may cause isolation. In conclusion, it has both advantages and disadvantages. | The elimination of daily commutes not only reclaims hours for personal development but also significantly lowers corporate overhead. Yet this spatial decoupling often fragments team cohesion, suggesting that hybrid arrangements, rather than fully remote models, better balance efficiency with human-centric workplace dynamics. |

💡 **提分关键**：删掉 `Firstly/Secondly/In conclusion`，用**语义链条**代替：`This shift → Consequently → What remains overlooked is → Therefore`。

---

### 🧩 3. Grammar：目的性复杂 vs 句型堆砌

**7分**：会用定语从句、条件句、被动语态，但有时为“显复杂”而造长句，略显生硬。
**9分**：句式变化完全服务于**信息重心、语气强弱、学术严谨度**，结构精准如手术刀。

| 表达意图 | Band 7 | Band 9 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| 强调政策必要性 | If the government does not invest in green energy, pollution will get worse. | Were policymakers to neglect renewable infrastructure, the compounding effects of environmental degradation would soon outpace remediation efforts. |
| 描述数据趋势 | The number of users increased quickly from 2010 to 2020. | User adoption experienced exponential growth over the decade, peaking in 2018 before plateauing. |

💡 **提分关键**：掌握3种“学术级语法工具”并自然嵌入：

1. **分词短语作状语**：`Driven by demographic shifts, urban centers are...`
2. **名词化结构**：`The implementation of stricter regulations led to...`（代替 `When they implemented stricter rules, it caused...`）
3. **条件倒装/虚拟**：`Had the initiative been launched earlier, outcomes would likely have differed.`

---

### 🎯 4. Lexical Resource：地道搭配 vs 同义词替换

**7分**：知道高级词，但常犯 `collocation error`（搭配错误）或 `register mismatch`（语体不符）。
**9分**：用词如母语者般“条件反射”，精准匹配语境、情感色彩与学术规范。

| 常见表达 | Band 7（易错） | Band 9（精准） |
|:---|:---|:---|
| 解决问题 | `solve the problem` / `make the problem better` | `address the issue` / `mitigate the challenge` / `alleviate the burden` |
| 很重要 | `very important` / `has a big role` | `plays a pivotal role` / `is indispensable to` / `carries significant weight` |
| 导致坏结果 | `cause bad results` / `make things worse` | `exacerbate the situation` / `yield adverse consequences` / `trigger a downward spiral` |

⚠️ **致命陷阱**：7分考生常把 `impact` 当动词滥用（`It impacts society`），9分更倾向 `shape / influence / reverberate through / exert a profound effect on`。

---

### 🗣️ 5. Speaking 专属：话语管理 & 语音特征

**7分**：能持续说，有 `well, you know, I mean`，语调清晰但缺乏层次。
**9分**：用**话语标记（discourse markers）**引导逻辑，用**重音/停顿/连读**传递态度，极少自我修正。

| 口语 Part 3 | Band 7 | Band 9 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| *How has social media changed friendships?* | It’s good for keeping in touch, but, um, people don’t meet face-to-face as much. So I guess it’s a bit mixed. | What’s striking is how it’s redefined proximity. You can maintain dozens of weak ties effortlessly, yet the depth of those connections often suffers. It’s less about losing friends and more about recalibrating what intimacy means in a digital age. |

💡 **提分关键**：

- 用 `That said / To put it another way / What’s often overlooked is / I’d argue that` 替代 `and / but / so`
- 重音放在**新信息/对比词/态度词**上，而非每个词平均用力
- 允许 0.5 秒思考停顿，但绝不填 `uh/um/like`

---

### 🛠️ 7→9 实战行动清单

| 维度 | 7分习惯 | 9分训练法 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **观点** | 直接给结论 | 加一层“边界条件”：`This holds true provided that... / Beyond a certain threshold, however...` |
| **衔接** | 堆连接词 | 写完后删掉所有 `Firstly/Moreover/In conclusion`，看逻辑是否依然成立 |
| **语法** | 追求长句 | 每段刻意嵌入1个分词结构+1个名词化短语+1个条件/让步倒装 |
| **词汇** | 查同义词词典 | 建立“搭配库”：记 `动词+名词` / `形容词+名词` 组合，而非孤立单词 |
| **口语** | 怕停顿 | 录音后标注：删掉所有填充词，用 `That said / Precisely / To clarify` 替代 |

---

### 💎 一句话总结

> **7分是“把话说对”，9分是“把话说透且不留痕迹”。**
> 考官不看你用了多少难词，而看你能否用**最精准的语言结构，承载最清晰的逻辑层次**。弱语只是表象，底层是思维密度与语言控制力的双重升级。

# 雅思 7分 vs 9分：除弱语之外的10大核心差距

---

## 一、句子结构 (Syntax) — 9分的"建筑感"

**7分**：能用复杂句，但结构单一（多用 which/that 定语从句）。

**9分**：句式像交响乐，有主有次，长短交错，节奏感极强。

### 实例对比

**话题：城市化对环境的影响**

> **❌ Band 7**（能写对，但读起来"平"）：
> Cities are growing rapidly, which causes a lot of pollution, and this is bad for the environment, so governments should try to reduce it by building more parks and encouraging public transport.

> **✅ Band 9**（句子有"呼吸感"）：
> As urbanization accelerates, environmental degradation has become **an unavoidable consequence** — **one that** demands immediate policy intervention. **Rather than** merely expanding green spaces, governments **must fundamentally reimagine** urban infrastructure by prioritizing public transit and enforcing stricter emission standards.

### 关键技术差异

| 技术点 | ❌ Band 7 | ✅ Band 9 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **句式起点** | 每句以主语开头（Cities are... / People think...） | 以**状语、分词、倒装**开头（Rather than... / Not only has... / Were governments to...） |
| **连接方式** | 依赖 and / but / so 串联 | 用**破折号、分号、同位语、插入语**创造层次 |
| **主从关系** | 从句和主句"平起平坐" | **主句承载核心观点，从句提供支撑**，主次分明 |

---

## 二、用词精度 (Lexical Precision) — 9分的"手术刀"

**7分**：用"意思对的词"（approximately right）。

**9分**：用"唯一正确的词"（the only right word）。

### 实例对比

**话题：科技对就业的影响**

| 概念 | ❌ Band 7 用词 | ✅ Band 9 用词 | 差距分析 |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| 很多人失业 | **Many people lost their jobs** | **A substantial proportion of the workforce has been displaced** | "displaced" 比 "lost jobs" 精确——它暗示结构性替代，而非个人失败 |
| 电脑代替人 | **Computers replaced workers** | **Automation has rendered human labor redundant in certain sectors** | "rendered redundant" 是经济学标准表达 |
| 政府应该帮助 | **The government should help these people** | **The government bears a responsibility to facilitate workforce retraining** | "facilitate workforce retraining" 是具体政策语言 |
| 这是个大问题 | **This is a big problem** | **This constitutes a systemic socioeconomic challenge** | "systemic" 说明问题的结构性 |

---

## 三、搭配 (Collocations) — 9分的"母语感"

**7分**：语法正确，但搭配"外国人味"。

**9分**：搭配完全符合英语母语者的自然习惯。

### 实例对比

| ❌ Band 7（语法对，搭配错） | ✅ Band 9（母语级搭配） |
|:---|:---|
| **do** a mistake | **make** a mistake |
| **make** research | **conduct / undertake** research |
| **strong** arguments | **compelling / cogent** arguments |
| **increase** the knowledge | **broaden / enhance** the knowledge |
| **pay** attention | **draw** attention |
| **deep** influence | **profound** influence |
| **enhance** the risk | **mitigate / exacerbate** the risk |
| **open** a debate | **spark / ignite** a debate |
| **have** a trend | **reflect / underscore** a trend |

> **💡 核心规律**：7分考生把英语当"乐高积木"拼（语法规则对就行）；9分考生把英语当"肌肉记忆"用（每个词都有固定的"伴侣"）。

---

## 四、连贯与衔接 (Coherence & Cohesion) — 9分的"逻辑链"

**7分**：用连接词串联（Firstly, Secondly, In conclusion）。

**9分**：用**逻辑本身**推动行文，连接词退居二线。

### 实例对比

**话题：政府是否应该资助艺术？**

> **❌ Band 7**（"路标式"写作，像流水线）：
> Firstly, art is important for culture. Secondly, it creates jobs. However, some people think the money should be spent on other things. In conclusion, I believe the government should fund the arts.

> **✅ Band 9**（逻辑自己会"走路"）：
> The arts serve as the cultural fabric of a society — **without which** national identity loses its distinctiveness. **Beyond cultural preservation**, the creative industries generate substantial employment, particularly in urban economies where they account for a growing share of GDP. **That said**, fiscal constraints are real: **redirecting** arts funding toward healthcare or infrastructure **may yield more immediate returns**. **Nevertheless**, the long-term erosion of cultural capital poses a risk **far graver than** short-term budget deficits suggest — **a trade-off** policymakers cannot afford to ignore.

### 关键差异

| 维度 | ❌ Band 7 | ✅ Band 9 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **段落推进** | 靠"Firstly / Secondly"标记 | 靠**因果、对比、让步**的内在逻辑推进 |
| **转折方式** | "However" 一刀切 | 用 **That said / Nevertheless / On the other hand** 等多维度转折 |
| **段尾-段首衔接** | 每段独立，像"列表" | 前段结论 = 后段前提，像**链条** |

## 五、论证深度 (Task Response) — 9分的"思维层次"

**7分**：有观点，有论据，但停留在**一层**。

**9分**：有观点 → 有论据 → **有让步 → 有反转 → 有更高层结论**。

### 实例对比

**写作题目：Some people think the best way to reduce crime is to give longer prison sentences. To what extent do you agree?**

> **❌ Band 7 论证（单层）**：
> I disagree. Longer prison sentences do not reduce crime because criminals may not think about the consequences. **Instead**, education and job opportunities are better solutions because they address the root cause.

> **✅ Band 9 论证（多层）**：
> While longer prison sentences may **appear to serve as a deterrent**, the evidence suggests otherwise: recidivism rates remain stubbornly high in countries with the harshest sentencing guidelines, **indicating that** incarceration alone fails to address the behavioral and socioeconomic drivers of crime. **That is not to say** punishment is irrelevant — **it is**. A custodial sentence **undeniably serves the dual purpose of** protecting the public and upholding justice. **However**, if the primary objective is crime *reduction* rather than retribution, resources **would be far more effectively allocated** toward rehabilitation programs, vocational training, and community-based intervention. **Only when** punitive measures are paired with preventive strategies can societies hope to break the cycle of recidivism.

### 思维层次对比

```
Band 7 思维：A对 → B更好 → 所以选B
Band 9 思维：A看似对 → 但证据不支持 → 不过A有部分道理 → 
           然而如果目标是X → 那B+结合A才是最优解 → 
           只有两者结合才能根本解决问题
```

## 六、时态与语法精度 — 9分的"零失误"

**7分**：90%正确，偶尔出错。

**9分**：99%+正确，且能在复杂句中精准运用虚拟语气、分词结构等。

### 易错对比表

| 语法点 | ❌ Band 7 常见错误 | ✅ Band 9 正确表达 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **虚拟语气** | If the government **will spend** more... | If the government **were to invest** more... |
| **过去分词做状语** | **Comparing to** the past, life is easier. | **Compared to** the past, life is easier. |
| **悬垂分词** | **Walking down** the street, **the buildings** were beautiful. | **Walking down** the street, **I noticed** the beautiful buildings. |
| **倒装** | Not only it affects economy, but also society. | Not only **does it affect** the economy, **but it also** impacts society. |
| **关系代词** | The reason is **because**... | The reason is **that**... |
| **冠词** | **The** life is hard. | **Life** is hard.（泛指不加冠词） |

## 七、[[语域切换 (Register)]] — 9分的"变色龙"

**7分**：整篇一个调子（通常偏口语或偏生硬）。

**9分**：根据语境自然切换正式/非正式、学术/日常。

### 实例：口语 Part 1 vs Part 3

> **Part 1（日常闲聊）**：
> "Oh, I'm **absolutely obsessed with** cooking — I probably spend **way too much** time scrolling through recipe videos at midnight!"

> **Part 3（学术讨论）**：
> "The correlation between social media usage and declining attention spans is **well-documented**, yet policymakers have been **remarkably slow** to respond."

**关键**：9分考生在同一个考试中能自如切换两种语域——Part 1 像朋友聊天，Part 3 像大学研讨。

## 八、发音与节奏 (Pronunciation) — 9分的"音乐性"

| 维度 | ❌ Band 7 | ✅ Band 9 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **重音** | 每个词重音均匀 | 句子重音落在**实词**上（content words） |
| **弱读** | 每个音节都发清楚 | 功能词自然弱读（can → /kən/，and → /ən/） |
| **语调** | 偏平，或每句结尾都升调 | 陈述句降调、列举项升调、转折处语调变化 |
| **连读** | 词与词之间有明显停顿 | 自然连读（want to → wanna，kind of → kinda） |
| **停顿** | 在不该停的地方停（语法中断） | 在**意群边界**停（逗号、逻辑转折处） |

> **💡 一句话**：7分发音让你"听得懂"；9分发音让你"忘记他是非母语者"。

## 九、批判性思维 (Critical Thinking) — 9分的"辩证力"

**7分**：非黑即白（A好B不好）。

**9分**：看到灰色地带，给出条件性结论。

| 维度 | ❌ Band 7 | ✅ Band 9 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **立场** | I completely agree / disagree | **On balance, the advantages outweigh... provided that...** |
| **反驳** | Some people disagree, but they are wrong. | **Critics may argue that... However, this perspective overlooks...** |
| **限定** | This is always true. | **This holds true primarily in developed economies, where...** |
| **结尾** | In conclusion, I support... | **Ultimately, the question is not whether X is beneficial, but rather how to maximize its potential while minimizing its inherent risks.** |

## 十、自然语块 (Natural Chunks) — 9分的"预制件"

9分考生不是逐词思考，而是整块调用（chunk-based processing）：

| ❌ Band 7 逐词拼凑 | ✅ Band 9 整块调用 |
|:---|:---|
| This is a thing that is very common nowadays | **This is a widespread phenomenon** |
| People who are poor do not have the same chance | **Disadvantaged communities face unequal opportunities** |
| This problem is getting worse and worse | **The situation is deteriorating at an alarming rate** |
| We need to find the answer to this problem | **This necessitates a viable solution** |
| In the modern world today | **In contemporary society / In the modern era** |
