## 雅思语言维度全景

除了语域（Register），还有以下几个维度值得关注：

---

## 1. 搭配（Collocation）

[[雅思高频错误搭配对照表]]

比单词替换更重要。考官能立刻识别"中式英语搭配"。

|错误搭配|正确搭配|
|---|---|
|do a crime|commit a crime|
|make a research|conduct research|
|strong opinion|strong argument / firm belief|
|solve the pollution|address pollution|
|raise the economy|boost the economy|
|have influence to|have an influence on|

> 核心逻辑：英语词与词之间有"婚姻关系"，不能乱配。

---

## 2. 精确性（Precision）

用词模糊是中国考生最常见的扣分点。
[[雅思精确性对照表]]

|模糊表达|精确表达|
|---|---|
|There are many problems.|Unemployment and pollution are the most pressing concerns.|
|Technology affects our life.|Social media has fundamentally altered interpersonal communication.|
|People like money.|Financial incentives significantly influence individual decision-making.|

> 原则：能具体就不抽象，能量化就不泛指。

---

## 3. 名词化（Nominalization）

学术写作的标志性特征，把动词/形容词转成名词结构。
[[名词化实用详解]]

| 口语化（动词主导）                    | 书面化（名词主导）                               |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| The government decided to... | The government's decision to...         |
| People consume too much.     | Excessive consumption is...             |
| Technology developed fast.   | The rapid development of technology...  |
| We should educate children.  | The education of children is essential. |

---

## 4. 句式多样性（Syntactic Variety）

不是"用难词"，而是句子结构要有变化。
[[句式多样性实例]]

|类型|例子|
|---|---|
|简单句|Exercise improves health.|
|让步从句|Although exercise is beneficial, excessive training can be harmful.|
|条件句|Given that resources are limited, prioritization is essential.|
|强调句|It is sedentary lifestyles, not diet alone, that drive obesity.|
|倒装句|Not only does pollution damage ecosystems, but it also threatens public health.|
|分词短语|Having considered both sides, I believe...|

---

## 5. 态度标记（Hedging vs. Asserting）

写作中需要会"拿捏语气"，不能全程说死。
[[态度标记]]

|太绝对（扣分）|适当模糊|有力断言|
|---|---|---|
|Technology always helps.|Technology tends to improve...|Evidence strongly suggests...|
|Everyone agrees that...|It is widely acknowledged that...|Research consistently shows...|
|This is definitely wrong.|This approach may prove counterproductive.|This policy is fundamentally flawed.|

---

## 6. 连贯与衔接（Coherence & Cohesion）

这是雅思评分的独立维度，考的是逻辑流动，不是堆砌连接词。
[[连贯与衔接]]
**常见错误：**

- 每句都用 _furthermore / moreover_（机械堆砌）
- 段落内部逻辑跳跃，缺乏展开
- 代词指代不清（_it / they / this_ 不知道指什么）

**正确做法：**

- 用词语复现（lexical chain）代替连接词：_pollution → contamination → this environmental crisis_
- 段落结构：观点 → 解释 → 例子 → 归纳

---

## 优先级建议

|维度|对口语的影响|对写作的影响|难度|
|---|---|---|---|
|搭配 Collocation|⭐⭐⭐|⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐|中|
|语域 Register|⭐⭐⭐|⭐⭐⭐⭐|低|
|精确性 Precision|⭐⭐|⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐|高|
|名词化 Nominalization|⭐|⭐⭐⭐⭐|中|
|句式多样性 Syntax|⭐⭐⭐|⭐⭐⭐⭐|高|
|态度标记 Hedging|⭐|⭐⭐⭐|中|
|连贯衔接 Cohesion|⭐⭐⭐|⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐|高|

**最高ROI的练习**：先攻搭配（Collocation），错误最显眼、改正最快见效。
