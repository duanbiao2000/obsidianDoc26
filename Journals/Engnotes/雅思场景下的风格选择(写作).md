**底层需求：** 写作评分机制和口语不同，风格选择的逻辑需要重新推导。

---

## 先看写作和口语的根本差异

|-|口语|写作|
|---|---|---|
|评分容错|高，自然停顿可接受|低，每个词都留在纸上|
|风格偏好|Native naturalness|Educated formality|
|最大雷区|书面语带入口语|口语带入书面语|
|纽约式风险|低|**高**|
|伦敦式风险|中|低|

**结论先说：** 雅思写作的基础是**伦敦式**，但要避免伦敦式的两个陷阱。

## Task 1 和 Task 2 分开看

### Task 1 — 图表/流程描述

**风格：伦敦式学术变体，客观精准**

核心要求：

- 零个人观点
- 数据语言准确
- 趋势描述有层次

**关键句式库：**

|功能|表达|
|---|---|
|总体概述|"Overall, it is evident that..." / "The data reveals a clear trend toward..."|
|显著特征|"Most notably..." / "Of particular significance is..."|
|对比|"While X saw a steady rise, Y experienced a marked decline."|
|数据引用|"accounting for approximately 40% of..." / "peaking at X before leveling off"|
|趋势描述|"a gradual yet sustained increase" / "a sharp and unexpected drop"|
|例外情况|"with the notable exception of..." / "X, however, bucked this trend"|

**示例：折线图描述**

❌ 纽约式（口语化，扣分）

> "So basically the numbers went up a lot from 2010, then kind of flattened out. China was way ahead of everyone else honestly."

❌ 中式堆砌

> "From the line graph we can clearly see that the data shows an increasing trend which is going up from the year 2010 to 2020."

✅ 伦敦学术式

> "The graph illustrates a divergence in trajectories across the three economies between 2010 and 2020. China's output rose sharply — nearly doubling over the period — while both the US and Germany exhibited more moderate, incremental growth(渐进增长). By 2018, the gap between China and its counterparts had widened to a degree that warrants closer examination(值得深入研究)."

**差距在哪：**

|要素|低分|高分|
|---|---|---|
|数据处理|照抄数字|数字+解读|
|趋势词汇|go up / go down|surge / plateau / taper off / rebound|
|句式|简单陈述|对比+分析+引申|
|收尾|没有|暗示意义，不下结论|

## Task 2 四段式结构的风格处理

### 引言段 — 伦敦式改写题目，纽约式亮立场

❌ 中式重复题目

> "Nowadays, more and more people think that technology has changed our life. This essay will discuss both sides."

❌ 纽约式太口语

> "Okay so here's the thing about technology — it's complicated. Some people love it, some people hate it. I'm going to break this down."

✅ 融合版

> "The rapid proliferation of digital technology has prompted considerable debate about its net effect(总体影响) on human connection. While some contend that it has fundamentally eroded meaningful relationships, others argue that it has expanded the possibilities for connection in ways previously unimaginable. This essay will argue that the answer lies not in the technology itself, but in the structural incentives that govern its design."

**关键技巧：**

- 第一句：改写题目，不重复原词
- 第二句：呈现两方，用对比句式
- 第三句：**亮出自己的角度**——这是纽约式锐度

---

### 主体段1 — 伦敦式展开，有层次

**段落公式：**

```
Topic sentence（立场）
→ Explanation（机制解释）
→ Evidence/Example（具体支撑）
→ Linked conclusion（回收主题）
```

❌ 薄弱版

> "Technology helps people communicate. For example, we can use WeChat to talk to friends. This is very convenient and saves time."

✅ 伦敦学术+纽约锐度

> "One of the more underappreciated effects of digital communication is its capacity to sustain relationships across geographical boundaries that would otherwise have atrophied. Consider long-distance friendships or diaspora communities — platforms that enable asynchronous, low-friction contact have allowed these bonds to persist in ways that were structurally impossible a generation ago. The argument, then, is not that technology replaces depth, but that it removes certain logistical barriers to maintaining it."

**词汇层次示例：**

|普通词|高分替换|
|---|---|
|help|facilitate / enable / underpin|
|show|illustrate / demonstrate / underscore|
|important|significant / consequential / pivotal|
|problem|challenge / constraint / impediment|
|change|transform / reshape / reconfigure|
|think|contend / argue / posit / maintain|
|many people|a growing body of opinion / an increasing proportion|
|because|given that / in light of / on account of|

---

### 主体段2 — 引入对立面，伦敦式处理分寸

**不是简单"on the other hand"，而是有层次的让步**

❌ 简单对立

> "On the other hand, some people think technology is bad. They say people are addicted to their phones."

✅ 伦敦式让步+纽约式反驳

> "That said, it would be intellectually dishonest to ignore the mounting evidence that certain platforms are engineered for compulsion rather than connection. The distinction matters: a tool that maximises engagement metrics is not the same as one designed to deepen relationships. Critics who point to rising rates of loneliness among heavy social media users are not wrong — though the causal arrow is more ambiguous than popular discourse tends to acknowledge."

**关键句式：**

|功能|表达|
|---|---|
|有条件让步|"That said..." / "It would be remiss not to acknowledge..."|
|引入反驳|"The distinction matters, however..."|
|限定对方论点|"Critics are not entirely wrong, though..."|
|指出复杂性|"the causal relationship is more ambiguous than..."|
|否定简单化|"popular discourse tends to oversimplify..."|

---

### 结尾段 — 纽约式收口，伦敦式余韵

❌ 中式重复

> "In conclusion, I think technology has both advantages and disadvantages. We should use it properly."

❌ 过度纽约式

> "Bottom line — technology isn't the problem. It's how we design it. Fix the incentives, fix the problem."

✅ 融合收尾

> "Ultimately, the debate over technology's effect on human connection risks asking the wrong question. The more productive inquiry is not whether technology connects or isolates, but whose interests are served by the platforms that mediate our social lives. Designed with genuine human flourishing in mind, these tools carry real promise. As currently constituted, that promise remains largely unrealised."

**收尾的三个层次：**

- 第一句：跳出二元框架（伦敦式智识姿态）
- 第二句：重新定位问题（纽约式锐度）
- 第三句：有条件的结论（伦敦式分寸）

## 雅思写作最常见的风格失误

### 失误1：纽约式口语渗入

|口语化|书面替换|
|---|---|
|a lot of|a considerable number of / substantial|
|kind of / sort of|somewhat / to a degree|
|thing|phenomenon / issue / dynamic|
|get|obtain / acquire / achieve|
|big|significant / substantial / considerable|
|okay / fine|acceptable / adequate|
|basically|fundamentally / essentially|
|you know|直接删掉|
|I think|It can be argued / This essay contends|
|etc.|展开说，不用etc.|

---

### 失误2：伦敦式用错——空洞冗长

**这是中国学生模仿伦敦式最容易掉的坑**

❌ 伦敦式用错（听起来高级，实际空洞）

> "It is undeniably the case that in the contemporary era of modern society, the phenomenon of technological advancement has had a not insignificant impact on the way in which human beings interact with one another on a daily basis."

✅ 同样意思，精简有力

> "Digital technology has fundamentally reshaped how people connect — a shift whose consequences remain deeply contested."

**空洞伦敦式的特征：**

- 双重否定滥用：_not insignificant_
- 冗余修饰：_contemporary era of modern society_
- 名词化堆砌：_the phenomenon of... the way in which..._
- 信息密度为零

---

### 失误3：立场模糊

雅思Task 2明确要求有立场，伦敦式的迂回在这里是减分项

❌ 过度伦敦式（没有立场）

> "There are merits to both perspectives, and it is perhaps not possible to reach a definitive conclusion on this matter."

✅ 有立场+有分寸

> "While both perspectives carry weight, this essay contends that the case for X is ultimately more compelling — not because Y is without merit, but because the evidence points consistently in one direction."

## 词汇风格的频谱

```
口语端                                    学术端
   |                                          |
get  →  obtain  →  acquire  →  procure  →  secure
bad  →  poor  →  inadequate  →  deficient  →  deleterious
show →  reveal  →  demonstrate  →  illustrate  →  exemplify
think → believe → argue → contend → posit
change → alter → modify → transform → reconfigure
```

**雅思甜蜜点：频谱中间偏右** 不要最左（口语），也不要最右（过度学术显得堆砌）
