
### 动词 + 名词搭配（最易出错）

#### crime / law / rule 类

|❌ 错误|✅ 正确|说明|
|---|---|---|
|do a crime|commit a crime|crime永远搭commit|
|make a crime|commit a crime|同上|
|stop crime|reduce / combat crime|stop太口语|
|break a law|violate / break the law|注意用定冠词the|
|do illegal things|engage in illegal activities|—|
|follow the rule|comply with / abide by the rules|rules一般用复数|
|obey the regulation|comply with regulations|—|

#### research / study 类

|❌ 错误|✅ 正确|说明|
|---|---|---|
|make a research|conduct research|research不可数，无冠词|
|do a research|carry out research|同上|
|make a survey|conduct a survey|—|
|do an experiment|conduct / carry out an experiment|—|
|research about sth|research sth / research into sth|research是及物动词|

#### problem / issue 类

|❌ 错误|✅ 正确|说明|
|---|---|---|
|solve the pollution|address / tackle pollution|pollution不是problem|
|solve the poverty|alleviate / reduce poverty|alleviate才是配贫困的词|
|solve the traffic|ease / relieve traffic congestion|—|
|deal with the problem|address / tackle the problem|deal with偏口语|
|avoid the problem|circumvent / mitigate the issue|—|

#### economy / development 类

|❌ 错误|✅ 正确|说明|
|---|---|---|
|raise the economy|boost / stimulate the economy|raise不搭economy|
|develop the economy|develop经济可以，但更地道：foster economic growth|—|
|make economic growth|achieve / sustain economic growth|make不搭growth|
|increase employment|boost employment / create jobs|—|
|reduce unemployment|tackle / address unemployment|—|

#### 其他高频动词搭配

|❌ 错误|✅ 正确|说明|
|---|---|---|
|make influence|have an influence on|influence搭have|
|have influence to|have an influence on|介词错误|
|play an important role to|play an important role in|介词in|
|make contribution to|make a contribution to|需要冠词a|
|provide people benefits|provide benefits for people|语序错误|
|cause damage to environment|cause damage to the environment|需要定冠词|
|meet the demand of|meet the demand for|介词for|
|pay attention for|pay attention to|介词to|

### 核心规律总结

|规律|例子|
|---|---|
|**make** 搭"创造性产出"|make a decision, make progress, make an effort|
|**do** 搭"行为活动"|do research, do homework, do business|
|**conduct** 搭"正式活动"|conduct research, conduct a survey, conduct an interview|
|**commit** 搭"负面行为"|commit a crime, commit fraud, commit suicide|
|**cause** 搭"负面结果"|cause damage, cause harm, cause pollution|
|**raise vs. increase**|raise awareness / prices；increase rate / number|
|**heavy/strong/high**|heavy rain/traffic；strong wind/argument；high pressure/speed|

> **备考建议**：搭配错误比语法错误更难被自己发现，建议用《Oxford Collocations Dictionary》查每个核心名词的动词搭配，而不是靠语感猜。
