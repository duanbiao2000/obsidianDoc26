## 名词化（Nominalization）实例详解

名词化的本质：把**动作/状态**转移到**名词**上，让句子重心从"谁做了什么"转向"什么现象/过程/结果"。

### 三、句子级别转换实例

#### 教育类

|❌ 口语化|✅ 名词化|
|---|---|
|Children learn better when teachers use interactive methods.|The adoption of interactive teaching methods enhances children's learning outcomes.|
|If we educate people about nutrition, they will be healthier.|Nutritional education plays a pivotal role in improving public health.|
|Students drop out of school because they are poor.|Poverty-driven school dropout rates remain a persistent challenge in developing nations.|
|Teachers should encourage students to think critically.|The cultivation of critical thinking skills is central to modern educational philosophy.|
|When governments invest in education, the economy grows.|Government investment in education correlates strongly with sustained economic growth.|

#### 环境类

|❌ 口语化|✅ 名词化|
|---|---|
|Factories pollute the air and this causes health problems.|Industrial air pollution poses significant risks to respiratory health.|
|As the climate changes, more species will go extinct.|Accelerating climate change is driving unprecedented rates of species extinction.|
|People consume too much plastic and this damages oceans.|Excessive plastic consumption has caused irreversible damage to marine ecosystems.|
|When forests are cut down, carbon is released into the atmosphere.|Deforestation results in the large-scale release of stored carbon into the atmosphere.|
|If temperatures rise, sea levels will go up.|Rising global temperatures are projected to accelerate sea level rise significantly.|

#### 经济类

|❌ 口语化|✅ 名词化|
|---|---|
|When countries trade with each other freely, they grow richer.|Free trade between nations facilitates economic growth and wealth accumulation.|
|Rich people have more opportunities than poor people.|Wealth disparities translate directly into unequal access to educational and professional opportunities.|
|If wages don't go up, workers will be dissatisfied.|Wage stagnation breeds widespread worker dissatisfaction and reduced productivity.|
|Companies move to countries where labor is cheap.|The relocation of corporations to low-wage economies reflects global cost-minimization strategies.|
|When unemployment goes up, crime usually goes up too.|Rising unemployment rates are statistically associated with increases in criminal activity.|

#### 科技类

|❌ 口语化|✅ 名词化|
|---|---|
|As technology develops, many jobs will disappear.|Rapid technological development threatens the viability of numerous traditional occupations.|
|People use social media too much and feel lonely.|Excessive social media use has been linked to heightened feelings of social isolation.|
|AI is changing how doctors diagnose diseases.|The integration of AI into clinical practice is transforming diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.|
|When machines replace workers, inequality gets worse.|Automation-driven workforce displacement exacerbates pre-existing socioeconomic inequality.|
|Young people are addicted to their phones.|Smartphone dependency among adolescents represents a growing public health concern.|

#### 社会类

|❌ 口语化|✅ 名词化|
|---|---|
|More people are moving to cities and this creates problems.|Rapid urbanization generates substantial infrastructural and social strain.|
|Old people need more care as the population ages.|Population aging necessitates a fundamental reorientation of healthcare and social welfare systems.|
|When immigrants come, they bring new ideas and skills.|The influx of immigrants contributes to cultural diversification and skills transfer.|
|Crime goes up when communities are poor and have no hope.|Socioeconomic deprivation and diminished social mobility are primary drivers of rising crime rates.|
|Women still don't get paid as much as men for the same work.|The persistent gender pay gap reflects deep-rooted structural inequalities within labor markets.|

### 四、名词化的高级用法

#### 1. 用名词化压缩背景信息

|普通写法|名词化压缩|
|---|---|
|The government decided to increase taxes. This decision was controversial.|The government's controversial decision to raise taxes sparked widespread public debate.|
|Scientists discovered that the drug was effective. This discovery changed treatment.|The discovery of the drug's efficacy revolutionized treatment protocols.|
|People are becoming more aware of environmental issues. This awareness is growing.|Growing environmental awareness has shifted consumer behavior and policy priorities.|

#### 2. 用名词化做段落主题句

|弱主题句|强主题句（名词化）|
|---|---|
|Technology is changing education.|The digitalization of education represents one of the most consequential shifts in modern pedagogy.|
|Immigration has good and bad sides.|The economic and cultural implications of immigration remain deeply contested in policy discourse.|
|Exercise helps people stay healthy.|The relationship between regular physical activity and long-term health outcomes is well established.|

#### 3. 名词化 + 后置定语，扩展信息密度

```
基础句：
Governments should reduce carbon emissions.

名词化扩展：
The reduction of carbon emissions through carbon taxation
and investment in renewable infrastructure
represents the most viable pathway to climate stabilization.

结构拆解：
主语   = The reduction of carbon emissions（名词化）
修饰1  = through carbon taxation（方式）
修饰2  = and investment in renewable infrastructure（并列）
谓语   = represents
宾语   = the most viable pathway to climate stabilization（名词化）
```

### 五、名词化常用框架句式

|框架|例句|
|---|---|
|The + 名词化 + of + 领域 + is/remains...|The commodification of education is a growing concern among scholars.|
|名词化 + has + 过去分词...|Rapid urbanization has strained municipal infrastructure across Asia.|
|The + 名词化 + between A and B...|The correlation between income inequality and social mobility has been extensively documented.|
|名词化 + poses / presents + 挑战/机遇|Climate-induced migration poses unprecedented challenges to border policy.|
|名词化 + underlies / drives / shapes...|Structural inequality underlies many of the social problems discussed in this essay.|
|The + 名词化 + of A + on B...|The impact of automation on low-skilled employment warrants urgent policy attention.|

### 核心注意点

| 问题             | 说明                                                                            |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **不要过度使用**     | 每句话都名词化会让文章难读，口语化动词穿插其中反而更有节奏                                                 |
| **名词化不等于堆砌长词** | _The implementation of the utilization of resources_ 是错误示范                    |
| **配合精确动词**     | 名词做主语后，谓语动词要精确：_poverty drives / underlies / compounds_，而不是 _poverty affects_ |
| **雅思口语不适用**    | 口语中名词化过多反而显得不自然，口语保持动词主导即可                                                    |
