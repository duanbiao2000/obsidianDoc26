## 连贯与衔接（Coherence & Cohesion）

核心区别先说清楚：

|-|Coherence（连贯）|Cohesion（衔接）|
|---|---|---|
|**是什么**|逻辑是否流畅、论证是否合理|语言手段是否把句子粘合在一起|
|**考官怎么看**|读完段落能否理解你的逻辑链|句子之间的过渡是否自然|
|**常见错误**|观点跳跃、论证缺环、例子跑题|滥用连接词、代词指代不清|
|**本质**|思维问题|语言问题|

> 很多考生以为多用 _furthermore / moreover_ 就能拿高分——错。Cohesion 考的是**整体粘合机制**，连接词只是其中一种。

---

### 一、连接词（Connectives）：用对比用多更重要

#### 常见连接词的精确语义

中国考生最大的问题是把所有连接词当作"另外"来用。

|功能|连接词|精确含义|错误用法示例|
|---|---|---|---|
|**递进**|furthermore|在已有论点基础上加强|用在两个无关论点之间❌|
|**递进**|moreover|补充更重要的信息|与furthermore混用❌|
|**递进**|in addition|平行补充，无强弱之分|段首连用furthermore + moreover❌|
|**转折**|however|直接反驳或对比|用在让步后的轻微调整❌|
|**转折**|nevertheless|承认让步后坚持立场|与however混用❌|
|**转折**|nonetheless|语气比nevertheless略轻|—|
|**转折**|yet|简洁有力的转折，偏文学|用在学术论证中显突兀❌|
|**因果**|therefore|直接逻辑推论|用在没有因果关系的句子❌|
|**因果**|consequently|强调结果是自然发生的|—|
|**因果**|hence|正式，多用于数学/逻辑推论|口语中使用❌|
|**因果**|as a result|强调结果，语气中性|—|
|**对比**|in contrast|两个对立事物直接对比|用在相似事物之间❌|
|**对比**|by contrast|同上，更简洁|—|
|**对比**|whereas|同句内对比两个从句|用在句首独立使用❌|
|**对比**|while|同句内对比，语气较轻|—|
|**让步**|admittedly|承认对方观点有一定道理|用在自己完全同意的观点❌|
|**让步**|granted that|正式让步，后接从句|—|
|**例证**|for instance|举具体例子|用在抽象说明❌|
|**例证**|for example|同上，更通用|—|
|**例证**|to illustrate|用来阐明机制，不只是举例|—|
|**总结**|in conclusion|全文总结，只用于结尾段|用在主体段❌|
|**总结**|in summary|概括要点|—|
|**总结**|ultimately|归根结底，强调核心|—|

---

#### 连接词滥用 vs 精准使用

| ❌ 滥用（机械堆砌）                                                                                    | ✅ 精准使用                                                                                                                                                          |
| --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Firstly... Secondly... Thirdly... Furthermore... Moreover... In addition... Finally...        | 段落用主题句统领，内部用词汇衔接，只在真正转折时用连接词                                                                                                                                    |
| Furthermore, some people like sport. Moreover, exercise is healthy.                           | Regular physical activity yields measurable health benefits; cardiovascular function improves, stress hormones decrease, and cognitive performance is enhanced. |
| However, technology is useful. However, it has problems. However, we should use it carefully. | Technology offers transformative potential; however, its benefits are contingent on responsible deployment and equitable access.                                |
| So, therefore, as a result, consequently, the economy grew.                                   | Investment in infrastructure stimulated domestic demand, consequently driving GDP growth of 3.2% over the subsequent decade.                                    |

### 二、词汇衔接（Lexical Cohesion）

这是考官真正想看到的高级衔接手段——**不靠连接词，靠词汇本身建立联系**。

#### 1. 词汇复现链（Lexical Chain）

同一概念用不同的词反复出现，形成语义线索。

**示例话题**：Urban poverty

```
poverty（引入）
→ financial hardship（换词）
→ economic deprivation（名词化升级）
→ this socioeconomic marginalization（指代 + 升华）
→ such conditions(状况)（概括指代）
→ these structural inequalities（抽象归纳）
```

**段落示范**：

> Urban **poverty** remains one of the most intractable challenges facing developing economies. **Financial hardship** prevents millions of city dwellers from accessing adequate housing, nutrition, and healthcare. **Economic deprivation** of this scale is not merely an individual misfortune but a systemic failure — **this marginalization** is reproduced across generations through underfunded schools and precarious employment. Addressing **such entrenched inequality** demands structural intervention, not charitable gesture.

---

#### 2. 上义词与下义词（Superordinate & Hyponym）

先用具体词，再用上义词概括；或先概括再举例。

|下义词（具体）|上义词（概括）|
|---|---|
|anxiety, depression, burnout|mental health conditions|
|theft, fraud, assault|criminal offenses|
|coal, oil, natural gas|fossil fuels|
|smartphones, laptops, tablets|digital devices|
|doctors, nurses, pharmacists|healthcare professionals|

**段落示范**：

> Prolonged exposure to social media has been linked to **anxiety, disrupted sleep, and diminished self-esteem** among adolescents. These **mental health consequences** are particularly acute among girls aged 12 to 17, a demographic that research consistently identifies as most vulnerable to image-based social comparison. Addressing these **psychological harms** requires coordinated action from platform designers, educators, and policymakers alike.

---

#### 3. 反义词对比衔接

|对比词对|衔接效果|
|---|---|
|urban ↔ rural|建立地理对比框架|
|short-term ↔ long-term|建立时间维度对比|
|individual ↔ collective|建立责任主体对比|
|cause ↔ consequence|建立因果框架|
|supply ↔ demand|建立经济分析框架|

**示范**：

> **Short-term** economic gains from deforestation are well documented; the **long-term** ecological and fiscal costs, however, consistently outweigh them. This tension between **immediate** profit and **sustained** viability lies at the heart of most environmental policy disputes.

---

### 三、指代衔接（Reference Cohesion）

代词和指示词把前后句子粘合在一起——但用错会导致指代不清。

#### 指代工具分类

|工具|例子|注意点|
|---|---|---|
|人称代词|they / it / them|确保指代对象唯一清晰|
|指示代词|this / these / that / those|this指代上文概念，those指代上文名词|
|指示名词短语|this phenomenon / these findings / such measures|最安全的指代方式|
|比较指代|the former / the latter|只用于两个对象|
|替代词|do so / one / ones|替代动词短语或名词|

#### 指代不清 vs 指代清晰

| ❌ 指代不清                                                                                                | ✅ 指代清晰                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Governments should invest in education. They should also reform healthcare. It will improve outcomes. | Governments should invest in education and reform healthcare simultaneously. **These dual investments** are likely to generate compounding improvements in human capital development.                                                            |
| Technology affects employment. This is a problem.                                                     | Automation is displacing low-skilled workers at an unprecedented rate. **This structural disruption** demands proactive retraining programs rather than reactive welfare payments.                                                               |
| Some people support the policy. Others don't. They have different reasons.                            | Proponents of the policy cite its long-term fiscal benefits, while opponents emphasize **its** disproportionate burden on low-income households. **These competing priorities** reflect deeper ideological divisions over the role of the state. |
| Education is important. It helps people. They get better jobs.                                        | Quality education enhances individuals' employability, equipping **them** with the analytical and technical competencies that modern labor markets increasingly demand.                                                                          |

### 四、段落内部结构（Coherence 的核心）

连接词和词汇衔接解决的是句间问题，**段落内部的逻辑架构**才是 Coherence 的真正考点。

#### PEEL 结构（最稳定的段落框架）

|层次|作用|信号|
|---|---|---|
|**P** — Point|段落核心论点|主题句，清晰断言|
|**E** — Explanation|解释机制/原因|this is because / the reason is that|
|**E** — Evidence|具体例子/数据|for instance / research indicates|
|**L** — Link|回扣主题/过渡|therefore / this demonstrates that|

#### 低分段落 vs 高分段落对比

**话题**：Should higher education be free?

---

**❌ 低分段落（逻辑跳跃，衔接靠堆连接词）**

> Higher education should be free. Furthermore, many students cannot afford tuition fees. Moreover, education is important for society. In addition, other countries have free universities. Therefore, governments should make universities free. Furthermore, this will help poor people.

**问题拆解**：

|问题|说明|
|---|---|
|主题句太弱|"should be free"没有说明理由框架|
|论点跳跃|从学费→社会重要性→别国做法，没有逻辑链|
|连接词堆砌|furthermore连用三次，语义重复|
|没有机制解释|为什么免费会帮助穷人？没有说明|
|没有回扣主题|结尾句没有呼应段落论点|

---

**✅ 高分段落（PEEL结构，词汇衔接，逻辑清晰）**

> **[Point]** Eliminating tuition fees at public universities would substantially improve social mobility by removing the financial barrier that disproportionately excludes low-income students. **[Explanation]** When higher education is contingent on an individual's ability to pay, talented students from disadvantaged backgrounds are systematically deterred — not by academic incapacity, but by the prospect of debilitating debt. **[Evidence]** Germany's abolition of university tuition fees in 2014 provides a compelling illustration: enrollment among students from non-academic households increased by 7% within three years of the reform, demonstrating that cost, rather than aptitude, had been the primary obstacle. **[Link]** This evidence suggests that free higher education does not merely redistribute opportunity — it expands the total pool of skilled graduates available to the economy, generating returns that far exceed the fiscal cost of the policy.

**结构拆解**：

|句子|技巧|
|---|---|
|句1 Point|名词化主语 + 精确动词 + 具体机制（removing the financial barrier）|
|句2 Explanation|条件句 + 破折号插入 + 强调句（not by...but by）|
|句3 Evidence|具体国家 + 具体数据 + 分词短语收尾|
|句4 Link|this evidence指代上文 + 不仅...而且结构 + 回扣经济论点|

### 五、段落间衔接（篇章级别）

段落之间的过渡不是靠 _firstly / secondly_，而是靠**逻辑呼应**。

#### 方法一：段尾预告下段

|当前段结尾|下段开头|
|---|---|
|...these economic benefits, however, must be weighed against significant social costs.|Chief among these social costs is the erosion of community cohesion that rapid urbanization tends to produce.|
|...the individual benefits of higher education are thus well established.|The societal returns on educational investment are equally compelling, though they operate through different mechanisms.|

#### 方法二：下段开头回扣上段关键词

```
上段核心词：income inequality
下段开头：This widening inequality does not merely affect
         individual households — it fundamentally destabilizes...
```

#### 方法三：对比框架贯穿全文

|段落|框架词|
|---|---|
|主体段1|At the individual level...|
|主体段2|At the societal level...|
|主体段3|At the global level...|

或：

|段落|框架词|
|---|---|
|主体段1|In the short term...|
|主体段2|Over the longer term, however...|

### 六、全文综合示范

**题目**：Some believe that individuals are responsible for their own health, while others argue that governments bear primary responsibility. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

---

> **[引言]** The question of whether health outcomes are primarily shaped by personal choices or structural conditions reflects a broader ideological tension between individual agency and collective responsibility. **This essay argues** that while personal behavior undeniably influences health, the determinants of individual choice are themselves largely structural, making government intervention not merely justified but essential.

> **[主体段1 — 个人责任论]** Proponents of individual responsibility contend that lifestyle choices — diet, exercise, smoking, and alcohol consumption — are the proximate drivers of most chronic disease. **This position** has intuitive appeal: adults in democratic societies possess the cognitive capacity to make informed decisions, and **this autonomy** should carry corresponding accountability. Research consistently shows that behavioral modification yields measurable health improvements regardless of socioeconomic background, suggesting that personal agency retains significance even under adverse conditions.

> **[主体段2 — 反驳 + 结构性因素]** **This individual-centric framing**, however, obscures a critical reality: the choices available to individuals are profoundly constrained by the environments governments create or neglect. **Food deserts** — urban areas where affordable nutritious food is structurally unavailable — illustrate how **economic deprivation** shapes dietary behavior independently of individual preference. **Such structural determinants** of health are not amenable to personal willpower; **they** require policy intervention in the form of urban planning reform, subsidy restructuring, and healthcare access expansion. The robust correlation between postcode and life expectancy in virtually every developed nation confirms that **these environmental factors** outweigh individual choice as predictors of health outcomes.

> **[结论]** Ultimately, the dichotomy between individual and governmental responsibility is a false one. **Personal agency** operates within boundaries that policy shapes, and **those boundaries** are either expanded or contracted by political decisions. Governments that invest in preventive infrastructure, equitable food systems, and universal healthcare do not undermine individual responsibility — **they create the conditions** in which meaningful personal choice becomes genuinely possible.

---

**全文衔接机制总览**：

| 机制      | 实例                                                                                 |
| ------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 词汇复现链   | health → health outcomes → chronic disease → health improvements → health outcomes |
| 指示名词短语  | This position / This individual-centric framing / Such structural determinants     |
| 反义对比    | individual ↔ structural / personal ↔ collective / proximate ↔ structural           |
| 段落间回扣   | 段2开头"This individual-centric framing"直接指代段1核心论点                                    |
| 框架词对称   | individual level → structural level → policy level                                 |
| 连接词精准使用 | however（真正转折）/ ultimately（归纳）/ 无堆砌                                                 |
