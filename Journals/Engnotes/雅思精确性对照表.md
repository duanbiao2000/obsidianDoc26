## 雅思精确性对照表

核心问题：中国考生习惯用"大词"兜底，导致表达空洞。考官看到模糊句子会认为你**没有观点，只有套话**。

---

### 一、主语模糊 → 具体化

|❌ 模糊主语|✅ 精确主语|说明|
|---|---|---|
|People think...|Young people / Researchers / Policymakers argue...|"people"等于没说谁|
|Everyone knows...|It is widely acknowledged that...|避免过度概括|
|Society should...|Governments / Schools / Employers should...|society无法执行动作|
|We need to...|Individuals / Authorities need to...|we指代不清|
|They say that...|Studies suggest / Experts argue that...|they是谁？|
|The world is changing.|Globalization and digitalization are reshaping economies.|说清楚什么在变|
|Humans are destroying nature.|Industrial emissions and deforestation are degrading ecosystems.|说清楚谁在做什么|

---

### 二、谓语模糊 → 精确动词

|❌ 模糊动词|✅ 精确动词|适用场景|
|---|---|---|
|affects|undermines / reinforces / exacerbates / alleviates|说清楚是正面还是负面影响|
|changes|transforms / reshapes / disrupts / alters|说清楚变化方向|
|helps|facilitates / enables / empowers / accelerates|说清楚怎么帮|
|hurts / harms|damages / deteriorates / erodes / jeopardizes|说清楚程度|
|shows|demonstrates / indicates / reveals / confirms|说清楚数据/论据的作用|
|makes people|compels / motivates / discourages / prevents people from|说清楚是推动还是阻止|
|gets better|improves / recovers / stabilizes / rebounds|说清楚怎么好转|
|gets worse|deteriorates / worsens / escalates / compounds|说清楚怎么恶化|
|causes problems|creates barriers / generates inequality / triggers conflict|说清楚是什么问题|
|is related to|correlates with / stems from / contributes to|说清楚关系方向|

---

### 三、宾语模糊 → 具体化

|❌ 模糊宾语|✅ 精确宾语|说明|
|---|---|---|
|affects our life|affects career prospects / mental health / family dynamics|life太宽泛|
|solves the problem|reduces youth unemployment / lowers carbon emissions|problem是什么？|
|brings benefits|improves academic performance / boosts household income|benefits具体是什么？|
|causes damage|causes irreversible ecological damage / long-term health risks|damage的类型和程度|
|has an impact on society|widens the wealth gap / marginalizes low-income communities|society的哪个层面？|
|influences children|impairs cognitive development / increases screen addiction|children受到什么影响？|
|destroys the environment|depletes groundwater reserves / accelerates species extinction|说清楚破坏了什么|

---

### 四、形容词模糊 → 精确描述

|❌ 模糊形容词|✅ 精确替换|说明|
|---|---|---|
|many problems|rising unemployment, widening inequality, and urban congestion|列举具体问题|
|a lot of benefits|measurable economic benefits / long-term health outcomes|说清楚哪类好处|
|very important|critical to economic stability / central to social cohesion|important重要在哪？|
|very bad|financially crippling / psychologically damaging / environmentally catastrophic|坏在什么维度|
|big difference|a marked disparity in income / a significant gap in educational attainment|差距是什么的差距|
|some countries|developing nations / high-income economies / resource-rich states|some countries是哪些？|
|new technology|artificial intelligence / renewable energy / biotechnology|technology具体是什么|
|good education|equitable access to quality education / evidence-based pedagogy|教育好在哪里|

---

### 五、原因/结果模糊 → 机制清晰

|❌ 模糊因果|✅ 精确因果链|说明|
|---|---|---|
|Technology causes unemployment.|Automation displaces low-skilled workers faster than retraining programs can absorb them.|说清楚机制|
|Poverty leads to crime.|Financial desperation drives individuals toward theft and drug trafficking when legitimate opportunities are absent.|说清楚为什么|
|Education is important.|Access to quality education equips individuals with skills that increase employability and lifetime earnings.|重要在哪个环节|
|Social media is bad for teenagers.|Prolonged social media use displaces sleep and face-to-face interaction, which are essential for adolescent cognitive development.|具体哪里坏、为什么坏|
|The government should do something.|Governments should introduce progressive taxation to redistribute wealth and fund public healthcare.|做什么、怎么做、目的是什么|
|Exercise is good for health.|Regular aerobic exercise reduces cardiovascular disease risk by strengthening cardiac muscle and lowering blood pressure.|好在哪个生理机制|

---

### 六、时间/范围模糊 → 限定清晰

|❌ 模糊范围|✅ 精确限定|说明|
|---|---|---|
|In recent years...|Over the past two decades... / Since the 2008 financial crisis...|多近？|
|In some places...|In densely populated urban centers... / In sub-Saharan Africa...|哪些地方？|
|More and more people...|An increasing proportion of working-age adults...|什么人？|
|Most people think...|Survey data consistently shows that over 70% of respondents...|用数据锚定|
|In the future...|Within the next decade, if current trends continue...|什么时间维度的未来？|
|Many studies show...|Longitudinal studies conducted over 20 years indicate...|什么类型的研究？|

---

### 七、套话句式 → 实质内容

这是雅思 Task 2 最常见的"高分陷阱"——句子听起来像高分，实则空洞。

|❌ 套话/空洞|✅ 有实质内容|
|---|---|
|This is a controversial issue with both advantages and disadvantages.|While remote work increases individual flexibility, it risks eroding team cohesion and organizational culture.|
|With the development of modern society...|As urbanization accelerates and digital connectivity expands...|
|In today's fast-paced world...|In an era defined by automation and global competition...|
|We should pay more attention to this problem.|Policymakers must prioritize early childhood nutrition to prevent long-term cognitive deficits.|
|It is undeniable that technology plays an important role.|Algorithmic hiring tools have measurably reduced recruitment bias in Fortune 500 companies.|
|Different people have different opinions on this topic.|Proponents emphasize economic efficiency, while critics warn of deepening social stratification.|
|This problem needs to be solved as soon as possible.|Without immediate policy intervention, urban air pollution will exceed WHO safety thresholds in 60% of Asian megacities by 2030.|

---

### 核心训练方法

每写完一个句子，问自己三个问题：

|问题|作用|
|---|---|
|**谁在做？**|检查主语是否具体|
|**怎么做到的？**|检查机制是否交代清楚|
|**具体是什么？**|检查名词是否可以再具体一层|

> 精确性不是要你堆砌术语，而是**每个句子都能独立成立、有信息量**。模糊句子的本质是：去掉它，文章损失为零。
