"Try to identify a somewhat niche principle or idea from the discipline of economics. This should be a principle or idea that early undergraduates wouldn't have heard of but late graduate students would have. It should be a relatively obscure but interesting and useful _to know about nonetheless_. Once you have identified such a principle, think of a story that could be used to illustrate your chosen principle. This should be an illustrative 3 paragraph story that would fully explain the principle or idea you've chosen but without naming the principle or idea itself. You can then name the principle or idea at the end of the story and explain it and how it is illustrated by the story in a single paragraph."  
"试着从经济学中找到一个有点冷门但既有趣又实用的原则或概念。这个原则或概念应该是低年级本科生没听说过的，但高年级研究生会了解的。一旦你找到了这样的原则或概念，想一个故事来阐释你选定的原则或概念。这个故事应该是一个三段式的说明性故事，能完全解释你选定的原则或概念，但不要直接说出这个原则或概念。你可以在故事末尾命名这个原则或概念，并用一段话解释它以及故事如何说明它。"

好，现在需求清晰了。给你三个独立提示词：

---

## Prompt 1 — 寓言版本

```
Identify a niche economics principle that graduate students would know 
but undergraduates would not. It should be obscure yet genuinely useful.

Present it as a fable:
- Animal or archetypal characters (merchant, elder, farmer, etc.)
- 3 paragraphs: setup / contradiction / resolution
- The principle must emerge naturally from the plot — never named in the story
- Prose should be spare and parable-like, not novelistic
- Final paragraph: name the principle, define it precisely, explain how 
  the fable illustrates it

Constraints: No modern settings. No academic language inside the fable 
itself. Total length under 400 words.
```

---

## Prompt 2 — 雅思口语版本

```
Identify a niche economics principle that graduate students would know 
but undergraduates would not.

Then predict 1 realistic IELTS Speaking exam question per part that 
could plausibly appear in an actual test, chosen from the following 
topic domains — select the most relevant fit for the principle:

TOPIC DOMAINS (pick 1):
[ ] Technology & Media  [ ] Environment & Climate  [ ] Education
[ ] Work & Career  [ ] Health & Lifestyle  [ ] Society & Culture
[ ] Travel & Places  [ ] Family & Relationships  [ ] Government & Policy
[ ] Science & Innovation

Generate a full Band 9 model answer for each part:

Part 1 — Conversational, natural, 2-3 sentence answer
Part 2 — Cue card response, 150-180 words, storytelling register
Part 3 — Academic register, 2 questions, each answered in 3-5 sentences 
          with a clear claim, reasoning, and implication

The economics principle should subtly inform the answers without being 
named or made explicit. Answers must reflect native-level fluency: 
varied sentence rhythm, appropriate hedging, no textbook phrasing.

End with one paragraph naming and explaining the principle and how it 
surfaces across the three parts.
```

## Prompt 3 — 雅思写作版本

```
Identify a niche economics principle that graduate students would know 
but undergraduates would not.

Then predict 1 realistic IELTS Writing Task 2 question that could 
plausibly appear in an actual test, chosen from the following topic 
domains — select the most relevant fit for the principle:

TOPIC DOMAINS (pick 1):
[ ] Technology & Media  [ ] Environment & Climate  [ ] Education
[ ] Work & Career  [ ] Health & Lifestyle  [ ] Society & Culture
[ ] Travel & Places  [ ] Family & Relationships  [ ] Government & Policy
[ ] Science & Innovation

Generate a Band 9 model essay:
- 4 paragraphs: introduction / body 1 / body 2 / conclusion
- 260-290 words total
- Introduction: paraphrase the question + clear thesis in 2 sentences
- Body paragraphs: one clear argument each, with reasoning and 1 
  concrete example or mechanism — no bullet points, flowing prose
- Conclusion: synthesise, no new arguments, end with implication
- Register: formal academic, no contractions, no first-person except 
  where naturally appropriate
- The economics principle should drive the argument without being named

End with one paragraph naming and explaining the principle and how it 
structures the essay's central argument.
```

---

**使用说明：**

三个提示词完全独立——输入任意一个即可获得对应格式输出。如果你想控制生成同一个经济学概念的三个版本，在每个提示词开头加一行：

```
Use this economics principle: [概念名称]
```

即可锁定概念，格式由提示词控制。

[[the Endowment Effect]]
