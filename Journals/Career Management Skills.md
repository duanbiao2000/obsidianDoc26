芬兰的**官方框架**里，更常见的不是“20个技能模块”这个固定说法，而是“生涯管理技能（Career Management Skills, CMS）”和“终身生涯指导（Lifelong Guidance）”体系下的一组能力领域。芬兰国家层面的框架明确指出，它的作用是帮助人们在不断变化的学习与工作环境中，做出对教育和职业的知情决策 。peda+1

## 官方框架怎么看

芬兰的国家框架强调，生涯管理技能不是单一课程，而是一套贯穿学校、职业教育、就业服务和工作场所的能力体系。官方资料把重点放在“理解自己、理解教育与工作机会、规划未来、做出决定、适应变化和采取行动”这些核心能力上 。journals.sagepub+2

## 对应到能力领域

按芬兰官方材料可归纳为下面这些领域：

- 自我认知：识别自己的技能、兴趣、价值观和优势 。xycareer+1
- 学习与研究技能：会学习、会查找信息、会使用学习策略 。cedefop.europa+1
- 教育选择能力：理解不同教育路径、资格和升学可能性 。valtioneuvosto+1
- 职业与劳动市场认知：了解职业世界、行业变化和就业机会 。cedefop.europa+1
- 决策能力：能比较选项并做出可执行的决定 。peda+1
- 规划能力：制定短期和长期计划，持续调整目标 。valtioneuvosto+1
- 变化适应能力：应对转学、转岗、失业或重新学习等转衔情境 。journals.sagepub+1
- 行动与执行能力：把计划变成行动，申请、沟通、面试、完成任务 。peda+1
- 关系与协作能力：在学习、指导和工作环境中与他人合作 。[[journals.sagepub](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/2158244020968778)]​
- 终身学习意识：把职业发展视为持续过程，而不是一次性选择 。cedefop.europa+1

## 你提到的“20个模块”如何对应

如果你想把“20个技能模块”对齐到芬兰官方框架，可以理解为把上面的能力再细分成更具体的训练点，比如把“自我认知”拆成兴趣、性格、价值观、优势；把“决策能力”拆成信息收集、比较选项、风险判断、执行反馈。这样拆分后，数量上接近“20项”，但本质上仍然属于官方框架中的这些核心能力领域 。peda+1

## 一句话对应

最接近的官方表达是：**芬兰的生涯教育框架围绕“自我认知、学习能力、教育与职业信息、决策、规划、行动、适应变化和终身学习”展开**，而不是一个全国统一命名的“20个模块”清单 。valtioneuvosto+2

下面我按“能力领域”分组，每个给你一些可直接用作雅思口语/写作的开放式题目，方便你练习或做课堂活动。

---

## 自我认知

- Describe a time when you discovered a new strength or talent in yourself. What impact did it have on your future plans?
- Some people think knowing your personality is more important than knowing your skills when choosing a career. Do you agree or disagree?
- In your culture, how do young people usually find out what they are good at? Are these ways effective?

---

## 学习与研究技能

- Describe a learning strategy that has helped you study more effectively. How did you develop this strategy?
- “Learning how to learn is more important than what you learn at school.” To what extent do you agree or disagree?
- How has the Internet changed the way students search for and evaluate information?

---

## 教育选择能力

- Describe an important educational choice you have made, such as choosing a major or a school. What factors influenced your decision?
- Some people believe that parents should make educational decisions for their children, while others think students should decide for themselves. What is your opinion?
- How important is it for schools to provide professional guidance when students choose their future study paths?

---

## 职业与劳动市场认知

- Describe a job that is becoming more popular in your country. Why do you think this is happening?
- Many traditional jobs are disappearing while new jobs are emerging. Is this change more positive or negative for young people?
- How well do you think schools prepare students to understand the real job market?

---

## 决策能力

- Describe a difficult decision you had to make about your future. How did you finally make up your mind?
- “Young people rely too much on others when making important life decisions.” Do you agree or disagree?
- What methods can people use to make better long‑term decisions about their education or career?

---

## 规划能力

- Describe a long‑term goal you have set for yourself. What steps are you taking to achieve it?
- Some people prefer to plan every step of their future, while others like to be flexible. Which approach do you think is better, and why?
- How can schools help students learn how to set realistic goals and follow them?

---

## 变化适应能力（转衔与不确定性）

- Describe a time when you had to adapt to a big change in your study or work life. What did you learn from this experience?
- In today’s world, people often change jobs or even careers several times. Is this a healthy trend?
- What skills do people need to cope with uncertainty in their education and career?

---

## 行动与执行能力

- Describe a project or plan that you successfully carried out from beginning to end. What challenges did you face?
- Some people say that having ideas is easy but taking action is difficult. Why do you think many people fail to turn their plans into reality?
- How important is self‑discipline for students who want to achieve their long‑term goals?

---

## 关系与协作能力

- Describe a time when working with others helped you solve a problem related to study or work.
- “Soft skills, such as communication and teamwork, are more important than technical skills in the modern workplace.” To what extent do you agree or disagree?
- How can group projects in school prepare students for real‑life cooperation in the workplace?

---

## 终身学习意识

- Describe someone you know who keeps learning new things throughout their life. What can we learn from this person?
- In the future, people may need to return to school many times during their career. Do you think this will be necessary?
- What can governments and companies do to encourage adults to continue learning?
