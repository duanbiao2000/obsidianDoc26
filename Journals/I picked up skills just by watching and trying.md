## 一、回忆 & 人生经历
1. **grow up**
   同义词：become older
   原句：I **grew up** in a family like this.

2. **look back on**
   同义词：remember
   原句：When I **look back on** those years, I feel lucky.

3. **go through**
   同义词：experience
   原句：We all **go through** difficult moments in life.

4. **miss out on**
   中文标注：错过
   音标：/ˌmɪs ˈaʊt ɒn/
   同义词：lose
   原句：I didn’t want to **miss out on** time with you.

5. **turn out**
   同义词：end up
   原句：It **turned out** to be one of my best decisions.
