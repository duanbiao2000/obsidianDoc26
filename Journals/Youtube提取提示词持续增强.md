**开场句（定锚）**

- `What is this video's central bet — and what would falsify it?`
- `What problem does the speaker think they're solving vs. what they're actually solving?`
- `What's the insight this video exists to deliver?`

---

**追问策略（三层递进）**

**第一层：逼出结构**

- `Map the argument as: Claim → Evidence → Assumption`
- `What's load-bearing vs. decorative in this argument?`

**第二层：制造摩擦**

- `What's the most charitable version of the counterargument?`
- `What evidence would change the speaker's mind — did they address it?`

**第三层：个人化吸收**

- `What would I do differently if I believed this completely?`
- `Where does this collide with something I already believe?`

---

最有效的完整链路：

> 开场用 `What's the central bet` 定主轴 → 中段用 `load-bearing vs decorative` 做减法 → 收尾用 `What would I do differently` 强制内化

这三步能把视频从「信息摄入」变成「认知更新」。

几个能瞬间拉高对话质量的开场句：

**逼出隐藏前提**

- `What are you assuming I already know?`
- `What's the question behind my question?`

**定位知识边界**

- `Where does expert consensus end and your inference begin?`
- `What would the strongest dissenting expert say?`

**压缩本质**

>- `What's the one thing that, if I understood it, everything else would follow?`

**时间维度**

- `What about this will seem obvious in 10 years but isn't yet?`

**元认知**

- `What's the most common way smart people get this wrong?`

最被低估的一句：

> **`What's the question I should have asked but didn't?`**

— 把对方的知识结构直接暴露出来。

几个经典的思维框架改造成提示词的方式：

**逼迫结构化思考**

- `What would a skeptic say?` — 强制生成反驳视角，防止笔记变成复读机
- `Steelman vs Strawman` — 同时输出最强支持论据和最弱攻击点

**麦肯锡/咨询系**

- `So what? So what? So what?` — 三层递进追问，把信息逼成洞察
- `What's the non-obvious takeaway?` — 过滤掉所有人都能说出的废话

**学术写作传统**

- `What assumption is this entire argument resting on?` — 找隐含前提，是藤校论文训练的核心动作
- `What does this contradict in your existing knowledge?` — 激活认知冲突，记忆锚定效果极强

**第一性原理系**

- `If this is true, what else must be true?` — 演绎推链，Munger式思维
- `What would have to be false for this to be wrong?` — 波普尔证伪框架直接入提示词

最高频被低估的一条：

> `Compress to one sentence a 5-year-old can understand, then one a PhD would respect.`

— 双端校验理解深度。
