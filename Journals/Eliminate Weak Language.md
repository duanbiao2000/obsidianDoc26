## **CH. 2 — Eliminate Weak Language** 消除弱语

**Core Rule:** Weak vocabulary = undisciplined thinking leaking through words

**Throw These Out:**

|✗ Weak Phrase|✓ Leader Replacement|
|---|---|
|"I think…"|"Here's what we're doing."|
|"Maybe we should…"|"We will."|
|"I'm not sure but…"|"Here's the direction."|
|"Hopefully…"|"We expect."|
|"Does that make sense?"|"Any questions?"|
|"I just wanted to…"|"I'm here to."|

**24hr Exercise:** Catch every weak phrase mid-sentence → stop → correct → say it stronger

**底层需求：** 用IELTS评分标准解析弱语问题，帮助考生理解"为什么扣分"而非只知道"不能用什么"。

---

## 雅思弱语：7分 vs 9分 对比

### 核心逻辑

考官评的不只是语法正确，而是**思维清晰度通过语言的投射**。弱语暴露的是：

- 论点没想清楚（所以用模糊词撑场面）
- 立场不敢明确（所以反复hedge）
- 缺乏论证信心（所以用filler填空）

---

### 一、Lexical Hedging 过度模糊化

|弱语（7分区间）|强语（9分区间）|
|---|---|
|_I think pollution is quite bad_|_Pollution is one of the defining crises of this century_|
|_It seems like technology might be helpful_|_Technology fundamentally reshapes how people access information_|
|_Perhaps governments should do something_|_Governments must mandate emissions caps — voluntary targets have consistently failed_|

**扣分原理：** Lexical Resource标准要求"precise and sophisticated use"。_quite / a bit / somewhat_ 是精确度的反面。

---

### 二、Circular Opening 无效开头

**7分考生的经典陷阱：**

> _"This essay will discuss whether social media has positive or negative effects on society."_

完全重复题目，**零信息量**，考官读第一句就知道你没观点。

**9分替换：**

> _"Social media has fractured public discourse more profoundly than any technology since the printing press — yet the mechanism is poorly understood."_

第一句就**建立立场 + 给出反常识角度**。

---

### 三、Filler Transitionals 假连接词

|弱语|问题|强语|
|---|---|---|
|_Also, furthermore, additionally_ （三个叠用）|没有逻辑递进，只是堆砌|_More critically / What follows from this is / The implications extend further_|
|_In conclusion, to sum up, in a nutshell_|只宣布结束，不推进论点|_The evidence points to one unavoidable conclusion:_|
|_As we all know_|无效填充，暗示你没有独立论点|直接陈述事实+来源逻辑|

---

### 四、Vague Quantifiers 模糊量词

**7分：**

> _"Many people believe that a lot of countries have some kind of problem with inequality."_

三个模糊词连用，整句话信息密度接近零。

**9分：**

> _"Wealth concentration has intensified across OECD nations over the past four decades, leaving the bottom quintile structurally excluded from economic recovery."_

**规则：** 凡用 _many/some/a lot of/various_ 时，问自己：**能不能换成具体的？** 能换就换。

---

### 五、Apologetic Register 道歉语气

这类弱语在口语Part 3最致命：

|弱语|9分替换|
|---|---|
|_I'm not really sure but maybe…_|_The evidence is mixed, but the stronger case is…_|
|_I don't know much about this topic but…_|直接给观点，不作免责声明|
|_Sorry, I mean…_ （自我打断）|用 _To be more precise_ 做主动修正|

考官要听的是**你如何在不确定中建立论点**，而不是你承认不确定然后停下来。

---

### 六诊断问：写/说完一句话后问自己

1. 如果去掉这句话，论点变弱了吗？→ 没变弱 = 这句是弱语填充
2. 这个词能换成更精确的吗？→ 能换就是弱语
3. 我在表达立场还是在回避立场？→ 回避就是弱语

---

**一句话总结：** 雅思9分的语言特征是**每个词都在承担重量**，弱语的本质是用词量换思维密度，考官一眼识破。

这是一个非常棒的切入点。你提供的 "Eliminate Weak Language" 原则不仅适用于商业领导力，在**雅思（IELTS）**考试中更是区分 **Band 6/7** 与 **Band 8/9** 的关键分水岭。

雅思评分标准（尤其是口语的 Fluency & Coherence 和写作的 Task Response/Coherence）非常看重**语气的确定性（Certainty）**和**观点的明确性**。低分考生往往习惯用“弱语”来给自己留后路，显得不自信；而高分考生则敢于直接陈述观点，用强硬的词汇支撑论点。

以下是雅思中常见的“弱语”雷区，以及 **Band 7（中等偏上） vs Band 9（专家级）** 的实战对比：

---

### 1. 观点陈述开头：去掉 "I think / I feel"
**弱语逻辑**：我不确定这是事实，只是我的个人感觉。
**强语逻辑**：这是基于分析的客观结论。

| 场景/话题 | ❌ Band 6-7 (Weak) | ✅ Band 9 (Strong) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **口语 Part 3: 科技** | **I think** technology makes people lazier because we don't move much. | **It is undeniable that** technology has fostered a sedentary lifestyle, leading to decreased physical activity. |
| **写作: 环保** | **In my opinion**, governments should spend more money on renewable energy. | **There is a compelling argument that** governments must prioritize investment in renewable energy sources. |
| **口语: 工作** | **I feel** that working from home is better for work-life balance. | **Working from home clearly offers** a superior work-life balance compared to traditional office settings. |

> **💡 替换公式：**
> *   I think → **It is evident / clearly / obviously that...**
> *   I feel → **I am convinced / It seems apparent that...**

---

### 2. 提出建议：去掉 "Maybe / Should / Hope"
**弱语逻辑**：这只是个建议，不一定对，别怪我。
**强语逻辑**：这是必须执行的解决方案。

| 场景/话题 | ❌ Band 6-7 (Weak) | ✅ Band 9 (Strong) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **写作: 解决交通拥堵** | We **maybe should** build more roads to solve the traffic problem. | **The most viable solution is to** expand road infrastructure to alleviate congestion. |
| **口语: 健康** | You **ought to** exercise more if you want to lose weight. | **It is imperative for individuals to** engage in regular physical exercise to maintain a healthy weight. |
| **写作: 教育** | Schools **hopefully** can help students find jobs. | Schools **have a critical responsibility to** equip students with employable skills. |

> **💡 替换公式：**
> *   Maybe we should → **It is advisable to / We must / The priority is to...**
> *   Hopefully → **Ideally / It is expected that...**

---

### 3. 结尾确认：去掉 "Does that make sense? / Any questions?"
**弱语逻辑**：我讲完了，但我不知道你听懂没，我很心虚。
**强语逻辑**：我的论证逻辑严密，结论不言自明。

*   **雅思口语**：考官不会因为你问 "Do you understand?" 就给你高分，反而觉得你在浪费时间或不自信。
*   **雅思写作**：结尾不需要问读者 "What do you think?"，而是要总结观点。

| 场景 | ❌ Band 6-7 (Weak) | ✅ Band 9 (Strong) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **口语结尾** | So, that's my idea. **Does that make sense?** | **In conclusion,** the benefits of global tourism far outweigh the drawbacks, provided it is managed sustainably. |
| **写作结尾** | **Do you agree?** Let me know in the comments. (这在雅思写作中是绝对禁忌) | **Given the evidence,** it is clear that immediate action is required to mitigate the effects of climate change. |

---

### 4. 模糊表达：去掉 "Very / Really / So / A lot of"
**弱语逻辑**：我找不到精准的词，只能用这些凑数。
**强语逻辑**：使用精准的学术词汇描述程度。

| 弱词 (Weak) | 强词替换 (Strong / Band 9 Vocabulary) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Very important** | **Crucial, Vital, Paramount, Indispensable** |
| **Really bad** | **Detrimental, Adverse, Severe, Detrimental** |
| **So big** | **Substantial, Significant, Overwhelming** |
| **A lot of people** | **A vast majority of the population, Numerous individuals** |
| **Good/Bad thing** | **Benefit/Drawback, Advantage/Limitation, Merit/Demerit** |

**实例对比**：
*   **Weak**: "Smoking is **very bad** for your health and causes **a lot of** problems."
*   **Band 9**: "Smoking has **detrimental effects** on health and is a **primary contributor to** numerous preventable diseases."

---

### 5. 被动与推诿：去掉 "It is said that / Someone says"
**弱语逻辑**：我不想为这个观点负责，是别人说的。
**强语逻辑**：数据/研究/事实支持这个观点。

| 场景 | ❌ Band 6-7 (Weak) | ✅ Band 9 (Strong) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **引用数据** | **People say** that the population is growing fast. | **Statistics indicate / Demographic data reveals that** the population is growing at an unprecedented rate. |
| **引出观点** | **It is often said** that art is subjective. | **It is a widely accepted notion that** artistic appreciation is inherently subjective. |

---

### 6. 实战演练：24小时修正法 (雅思版)

就像你提供的材料说的，你需要**在说出口/写下来的瞬间捕捉弱语并修正**。

**练习场景：讨论“远程办公的利弊”**

**你的第一反应 (Band 6.5 思维)**:
> "I think working from home is good, **but maybe** it makes people feel lonely. **I'm not sure if** companies should let employees do it forever. **Does that sound right?**"

**捕捉弱语**:
1.  ❌ I think
2.  ❌ but maybe
3.  ❌ I'm not sure if
4.  ❌ Does that sound right?

**强制修正 (Band 9 思维)**:
> "Working from home **offers significant flexibility**, **however, it inevitably** isolates employees. **Therefore, a hybrid model is the optimal approach** rather than a permanent remote setup. **This is the most sustainable strategy for modern businesses.**"

### 总结：雅思高分的“强势”心法
1.  **少用第一人称做主语**（I think, I believe），多用**It**做主语（It is clear, It suggests）。
2.  **少用情态动词表犹豫**（may, could, might），多用**肯定性动词**（is, causes, ensures）。
3.  **拒绝口语化填充词**（like, you know, sort of），哪怕停顿思考也比说这些强。
4.  **直接给结论**，不要在结尾反问考官。

**记住：在雅思里，你不是在“聊天”，你是在“做学术汇报”。汇报需要的是自信和精确，而不是客气和犹豫。**
