# 一、听力易听错（发音模糊/连读/生僻/近音）
这类词在听力里**弱读、连读、音节多**，很容易听岔。
1. **apnea** /ˈæpniə/
   易错：易听成 *appear / apple*
   原句：I got a surgery operation to cure my apnea.
2. **anesthetist** /əˈnesθətɪst/
   易错：音节太多，易听漏/听成 *artist*
   原句：what’s the name of the person who gives you the anesthetic? it’s the anesthetist.
3. **sedated** /sɪˈdeɪtɪd/
   易错：易听成 *related / seated*
   原句：Monday night I was totally sedated with the anesthesia.
4. **hypochondriac** /ˌhaɪpəkɒnˈdriæk/
   易错：长难词，听力完全抓不住
   原句：I don’t think I am such a Negative person and I’m not a hypochondriac.
5. **macabre** /məˈkɑːb(r)/
   易错：发音特殊，易听成 *microwave*
   原句：among these ugly liquids there were these Macabre things floating.

# 二、口语易读错/用错（发音混淆/词性错/形近音近）
这期Luke**专门强调**的易混词，是口语必考坑：
1. **muscle** /ˈmʌsl/
   易错：强行读出 /k/ 音 → 错读 /ˈmʌskl/
   原句：let’s say it wrong see what happens (muscle).
2. **breathe / briːð / (动词)** vs **breath / breθ / (名词)**
   易错：发音、词性混用
   原句：my breathing was out of control；let me catch my breath.
3. **desert /ˈdezət/ (沙漠)** vs **dessert /dɪˈzɜːt/ (甜点)**
   易错：重音、元音完全搞反
   原句：can you hear the difference between these words… desert and dessert.
4. **ship /ʃɪp/** vs **sheep /ʃiːp/**
   易错：短音 /ɪ/ 和长音 /iː/ 不分
   原句：can you hear the difference between these words… ship and sheep.
5. **work /wɜːk/** vs **walk /wɔːk/**
   易错：元音混淆，口语最常错
   原句：can you hear the difference between these words… work and walk.
6. **angry /ˈæŋɡri/** vs **hungry /ˈhʌŋɡri/**
   易错：开头 /æ/ 和 /ʌ/ 听不清、读不准
   原句：can you hear the difference between these words… angry and hungry.
7. **palate** /ˈpælət/
   易错：错读成 /ˈpeɪlət/（和 pallet 混淆）
   原句：they also cut below my nose to move the position of my palate.
