下面先给你从这期视频里挑出的、和“写作方法论/写作策略”直接相关的动词短语和词组，并附上原文例句，方便你做笔记或做 Anki 卡片。

---

## 和“备考/练习方法”相关

1. **prepare by practicing (writing essays)**
    
    - 原句：_“I highly recommend that you **prepare by practicing writing essays** because … it's the writing paper which gets the lowest score for most students.”_
        
    - 含义：通过大量练习写作文来备考。
        
2. **read some of the sample answers**
    
    - 原句：_“I recommend you **read some of the sample answers** that you can find on the Cambridge website and in the teacher's handbook…”_
        
    - 含义：阅读范文作为学习资源。
        
3. **get some ideas of what to do / what not to do**
    
    - 原句：_“There you'll **get some ideas of what to do**, you know, how to write a good essay or perhaps **what not to do** because some of the essays are not that great.”_
        
    - 含义：从范文中汲取“应该怎么写 / 不该怎么写”的思路。
        
4. **put yourself in the shoes of the examiner**
    
    - 原句：_“I recommend you **try to put yourself in the shoes of the examiner** and think about what they're looking for.”_
        
    - 含义：站在考官视角思考评分标准，这是很典型的“方法论”思路。
        
5. **identify, summarize and evaluate (key points)**
    
    - 原句：_“There will be two key points in each text which you need to **identify, summarize and evaluate**.”_
        
    - 含义：读题材时的三步：找出要点、概括要点、进行评价。
        
6. **underline / highlight the key points**
    
    - 原句：_“In your exam, I recommend you **underline** … the key points. In the computer-based exam, you can **highlight the key points** on the screen.”_
        
    - 含义：通过下划线或高亮来标记关键信息。
        
7. **use it as a reference**
    
    - 原句：_“Yeah, a really good essay. **Use it as a reference** if you like, but always keep in mind that it's not perfect.”_
        
    - 含义：把范文当作参考模板而不是完美答案。

## 和“完成任务/匹配评分标准”相关

1. **fulfil / complete the task**
    
    - 原句：_“The content criterion… focuses on how well the candidate has **fulfilled the task**.”_
        
    - 原句：_“…whether you've **completed the task** basically.”_
        
    - 含义：是否真正完成了任务要求。
        
2. **make sure (that) the target reader is fully informed**
    
    - 原句：_“It's about **making sure that the target reader is fully informed** and all the content is relevant to the task.”_
        
    - 含义：确保读者信息充分，这是内容得分的核心方法之一。
        
3. **use the appropriate register**
    
    - 原句：_“Communicative achievement focuses on … whether the candidate has **used the appropriate register**.”_
        
    - 含义：根据文体选择合适语体（正式/中性）。
        
4. **use the conventions of the task / essay writing**
    
    - 原句：_“…you must **use the conventions of the task**.”_
        
    - 原句：_“…whether you **followed the conventions of essay writing**.”_
        
    - 含义：遵循该文体的结构和格式惯例。
        
5. **demonstrate a wide range of … (vocabulary / grammar)**
    
    - 原句：_“You should be **demonstrating a wide range of advanced vocabulary** … and that you **use a wide range of grammatical structures**.”_
        
    - 含义：在作文中主动“展示”自己掌握的词汇和句型范围。

## 和“结构与组织方法”相关

1. **put together the piece of writing**
    
    - 原句：_“Organization focuses on the way the candidate **puts together the piece of writing**.”_
        
    - 含义：如何整体组织文章结构。
        
2. **have an introduction, two or three main body paragraphs, and a conclusion**
    
    - 原句：_“An essay should **have an introduction, two or three main body paragraphs, and a conclusion**.”_
        
    - 含义：典型议论文结构方案。
        
3. **support the internal organization (of the argument)**
    
    - 原句：_“Paragraph divisions clearly **support the internal organization of the argument**…”_
        
    - 含义：用段落划分服务于论证结构。

## 和“内容取舍与评价策略”相关

1. **develop (your) own ideas**
    
    - 原句：_“Here the candidate is really **developing their own ideas**. This is not really from the text.”_
        
    - 含义：在总结原文之外展开自己的观点。
        
2. **include (your) own ideas in (your) answer**
    
    - 原句：_“You need to paraphrase the information … and **include your own ideas in your answers**.”_
        
    - 含义：在转述材料基础上加入个人观点。
        
3. **focus on / develop the evaluation (of key points)**
    
    - 原句：_“I recommend you **develop the evaluation** and/or the summarizing of the key points and include fewer of your own ideas.”_
        
    - 含义：在写作时重点展开对要点的评价，而不是跑题扩展。
        
4. **avoid irrelevant content**
    
    - 原句：_“…and you've **completed the task without any irrelevant content**.”_
        
    - 含义：精简掉和任务无关的内容。

## 和“语言运用方法”相关

1. **use advanced / appropriate collocations**
    
    - 原句：_“They've **used a lot of really advanced and appropriate collocations**.”_
        
    - 含义：刻意选择地道搭配。
        
2. **paraphrase the information / content / key points**
    
    - 原句：_“Use your own words throughout as far as possible. So that's about **paraphrasing**. You need to **paraphrase the information and the content, the key points**…”_
        
    - 含义：通过改写来展示语言能力、避免抄原句。
        
3. **be consistent with (your) register**
    
    - 原句：_“It's really important to **be consistent with your register**.”_
        
    - 含义：语体在整篇文章中保持统一。
        
4. **be sparing in (your) use of rhetorical questions**
    
    - 原句：_“Be very **sparing in your use of rhetorical questions**.”_
        
    - 含义：修辞问句要少量、克制地用。
        
5. **use good punctuation**
    
    - 原句：_“You **want to use good punctuation**, but if it's just one or two misplaced commas, you're not going to get deducted marks.”_
        
    - 含义：重视标点作为提升“组织”和“清晰度”的工具。
        
6. **get penalized / get marked down (for mistakes)**
    
    - 原句：_“…then you're going to **get penalized**.”_
        
    - 原句：_“…I'm really quite surprised that the examiners didn't **mark this candidate down for** the lack of a conclusion.”_
        
    - 含义：因为某些写作问题在评分时被扣分。
