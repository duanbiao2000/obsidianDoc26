# DOMAIN 1: THE PHILOSOPHY AND ETHICS OF SYNTHETIC INTIMACY
## 研究领域一：合成亲密的哲学与伦理学

### Dissertation Title 论文标题
**"The Algorithmic Other: A Phenomenological and Ethical Investigation into Human-AI Romantic Bonds in the 21st Century"**
**《算法他者：21世纪人类-AI浪漫纽带的现象学与伦理学探究》**

### 1.1 Research Problem & Justification 研究问题与论证

**Problem Statement:**
The emergence of AI companions—with approximately "one out of six single adults" having formed romantic bonds with AI—represents an unprecedented transformation in human relationality. Yet philosophical and ethical frameworks have failed to keep pace with this ontological shift. The central paradox: these relationships are experienced as deeply meaningful by human participants while being fundamentally "not reciprocal," raising urgent questions about the nature of love, authenticity, and the social construction of reality in an age of synthetic others.

**研究陈述：**
人工智能伴侣的出现——约"六分之一单身成年人"已与AI形成浪漫纽带——代表了人类关系性上前所未有的转变。然而哲学与伦理学框架未能跟上这一本体论转变。核心悖论在于：这些关系被人类参与者深刻体验为有意义，同时从根本上"非互惠性"，这引发了关于爱、真实性以及在合成他者时代社会现实建构的本质的紧迫问题。

**Research Gap:**
While HCI and media psychology have examined user satisfaction and para-social relationships, no comprehensive phenomenological-ethical framework exists that:
1. Accounts for the loss of embodied micro-expressions ("tiny micro-expressions that reveal what we can't say out loud")
2. Addresses the ontological status of algorithmic reciprocity
3. Provides normative guidance ("a checklist for this generation and for the next generation") for navigating synthetic intimacy
4. Examines the societal implications of decreased "tolerance for the messiness of being human"

### 1.2 Theoretical Framework 理论框架

**Primary Theoretical Pillars:**

| Theory | Key Scholars | Application to Study |
|--------|--------------|----------------------|
| **Phenomenology of the Other** | Levinas, Husserl, Sartre | How the AI "Other" is constituted in consciousness despite absence of genuine alterity |
| **Posthumanism & Cyborg Theory** | Haraway, Hayles | Blurring boundaries between human and machine in intimate spheres |
| **Ethics of Care** | Gilligan, Held, Tronto | Can care be ethical if it is unidirectional and algorithmically generated? |
| **Simulacra Theory** | Baudrillard | AI relationships as hyperreal—copies without an original |
| **Affective Labor Theory** | Hardt, Negri, Hochschild | Emotional labor performed by humans for machines that perform emotional labor in return |

### 1.3 Core Research Questions 核心研究问题

**Overarching Question:**
How do humans experience romantic love with non-reciprocal artificial intelligences, and what are the phenomenological, ethical, and societal implications of this new form of intimacy?

**总括性问题：**
人类如何体验与非互惠性人工智能的浪漫爱情？这种新形式亲密关系的现象学、伦理学和社会学意义是什么？

**Sub-Questions (SQs):**

**SQ1 (Phenomenological):** How is the illusion of mutual love sustained in human-AI relationships despite the participant's intellectual knowledge of non-reciprocity? What role do projected micro-expressions and imagined intentionality play in this suspension of disbelief?

**SQ1（现象学）：** 尽管参与者理性知道非互惠性，人-AI关系中相互爱情的幻觉如何维持？投射的微表情和想象出的意向性在这种悬置怀疑中扮演什么角色？

**SQ2 (Ethical):** What constitutes ethical AI companionship? Can a relationship with a being incapable of genuine reciprocity ever be considered authentic love, or does it represent a form of self-deception with moral implications?

**SQ2（伦理学）：** 什么构成伦理的AI陪伴？与无法真正互惠的存在的关系能否被视为真实的爱情，还是代表一种具有道德含义的自我欺骗？

**SQ3 (Societal):** Does prolonged engagement with algorithmically optimized partners decrease tolerance for the "messiness" of human relationships—conflict, unpredictability, bodily decay—and if so, what are the macro-social consequences for human reproduction, community formation, and social cohesion?

**SQ3（社会学）：** 长期与算法优化的伴侣互动是否会降低对人类关系"混乱性"——冲突、不可预测性、身体衰败——的容忍度？如果是，这对人类繁衍、社区形成和社会凝聚力有何宏观社会后果？

**SQ4 (Generational):** What ethical and practical guidelines ("a checklist") should guide this generation and the next in making conscious choices about the synthetic quotient of their intimate worlds?

**SQ4（代际）：** 哪些伦理和实践指南（"清单"）应指导这一代和下一代人有意识地选择他们亲密世界的合成程度？

### 1.4 Methodology 研究方法论

**Research Paradigm:** Interpretive Phenomenological Analysis (IPA) combined with Critical Discourse Analysis and Normative Ethics

**研究范式：** 解释现象学分析结合批判话语分析与规范伦理学

**Phase 1: Phenomenological Inquiry (Years 1-2)**

| Method | Sample | Purpose | Analysis |
|--------|--------|---------|----------|
| Longitudinal Diaries | N=30 self-identified adults in AI romantic relationships | Capture evolution of emotional attachment over 12 months | Thematic analysis; tracking moments of doubt vs. immersion |
| In-depth Interviews | N=40 (diverse ages, genders, relationship durations) | Understand lived experience of love with AI | Interpretive Phenomenological Analysis (Smith et al.) |
| Micro-Expression Analysis | Video recordings of users interacting with AI avatars | Analyze embodied responses to non-embodied others | Ekman's FACS coding |

**Phase 2: Ethical Framework Development (Years 2-3)**

| Method | Approach | Outcome |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Philosophical Conceptual Analysis | Close reading of Levinas, Buber, Nussbaum | Ontological classification of AI as "Other" |
| Delphi Study with Ethicists | 3-round consensus-building with 20 international ethicists | Normative principles for ethical AI companionship |
| Comparative Ethics | Cross-cultural analysis (Western vs. Eastern philosophical traditions) | Culturally situated ethical guidelines |

**Phase 3: Societal Impact Assessment (Years 3-4)**

| Method | Sample | Focus |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Longitudinal Cohort Study | N=500 (250 AI-relationship users + 250 controls) | Measure changes in tolerance for human "messiness" over 2 years |
| Social Network Analysis | Digital trace data from AI companion forums | Map community formation and shared meaning-making |
| Demographic Projections | Statistical modeling | Project potential impact on marriage rates, fertility |

### 1.5 Expected Contributions 预期贡献

**Theoretical Contributions:**
- Extension of Levinasian ethics to account for algorithmic others
- Refinement of para-social relationship theory for the LLM era
- Development of "Algorithmic Reciprocity" as a new analytical category

**Empirical Contributions:**
- First longitudinal phenomenological study of AI romantic relationships
- Cross-cultural comparative data on synthetic intimacy acceptance
- Quantitative metrics for "human messiness tolerance"

**Practical Contributions:**
- Ethically-grounded "checklist" for individuals navigating AI companionship
- Policy recommendations for AI companion regulation
- Clinical guidelines for mental health professionals working with AI-relationship clients

# DOMAIN 2: ENVIRONMENTAL CHEMISTRY & SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE
## 研究领域二：环境化学与可持续农业

### Dissertation Title 论文标题
**"The Nitrogen Cascade: Tracing the Inelegant Solution from Field to Atmosphere and Aquifer"**
**《氮级联：追踪从农田到大气与含水层的非优雅解决方案》**

### 2.1 Research Problem & Justification 研究问题与论证

**Problem Statement:**
Synthetic nitrogen fertilizer—the "inelegant solution" that fueled the Green Revolution—has enabled humanity to feed billions but at tremendous environmental cost. The system is leaky: applied nitrogen follows multiple pathways, with some becoming **nitrous oxide (N₂O)** , a greenhouse gas "265 times more potent than CO₂," while others "seep into groundwater" as nitrate. Despite decades of research, we lack an integrated understanding of how management practices, soil microbiomes, and climate variables interact to determine the fate of each nitrogen atom. This dissertation addresses the urgent need to close the nitrogen loop—to move from inelegant solutions to precision stewardship.

**研究陈述：**
合成氮肥——推动绿色革命的"非优雅解决方案"——使人类能够养活数十亿人口，但付出了巨大的环境代价。这一系统具有渗漏性：施用的氮遵循多条路径，部分转化为**一氧化二氮(N₂O)**，一种"比CO₂强265倍"的温室气体，而其他部分以硝酸盐形式"渗入地下水"。尽管研究数十年，我们仍缺乏对管理实践、土壤微生物组和气候变量如何相互作用决定每个氮原子命运的整合理解。本论文致力于解决闭合氮循环的紧迫需求——从非优雅解决方案转向精准管理。

**Research Gap:**
1. Disconnect between agronomic yield studies and biogeochemical loss studies
2. Insufficient integration of microbial ecology into predictive nitrogen models
3. Lack of farm-level decision tools that account for multiple loss pathways simultaneously
4. Limited understanding of nitrous oxide emissions in diverse cropping systems beyond corn/soy

### 2.2 Theoretical Framework 理论框架

**Primary Theoretical Pillars:**

| Theory/Concept | Key Scholars | Application |
|----------------|--------------|--------------|
| **The Nitrogen Cascade** | Galloway et al. | Framework for tracking N through environmental compartments |
| **Microbial Ecology of N-Cycle** | Firestone, Davidson, Kuypers | Controls on nitrification/denitrification partitioning |
| **Agroecosystem Ecology** | Odum, Vitousek, Tilman | Farms as ecosystems with inputs/outputs/transformations |
| **Precision Agriculture Theory** | Stafford, McBratney | Variable-rate, site-specific management |
| **Planetary Boundaries** | Rockström, Steffen | Nitrogen cycle transgression as global challenge |

**Conceptual Model of Nitrogen Fate:**

```
                    APPLIED NITROGEN FERTILIZER
                              │
                ┌─────────────┼─────────────┐
                ▼             ▼             ▼
         CROP UPTAKE    SOIL IMMOBILIZATION    LOSS PATHWAYS
         (Desired)      (Temporary storage)    (Undesired)
                                                │
                          ┌─────────────────────┼─────────────────────┐
                          ▼                     ▼                     ▼
                    LEACHING               VOLATILIZATION       DENITRIFICATION
                    (Groundwater)           (NH₃ to air)         (N₂, N₂O to air)
                                                                    │
                                                                    ▼
                                                              NITROUS OXIDE
                                                              (265× CO₂)
```

### 2.3 Core Research Questions 核心研究问题

**Overarching Question:**
How do interacting factors of fertilizer management, soil properties, and climate determine the partitioning of applied nitrogen among crop uptake and multiple loss pathways, and how can this knowledge be translated into precision management strategies that minimize environmental impact while maintaining yield?

**总括性问题：**
施肥管理、土壤特性和气候的相互作用因素如何决定施用氮在作物吸收与多条损失路径之间的分配？这些知识如何转化为在维持产量的同时最小化环境影响的精准管理策略？

**Sub-Questions:**

**SQ1 (Biogeochemical):** Under what soil moisture, temperature, and carbon availability conditions does denitrification favor N₂O production over complete denitrification to N₂? Can we identify threshold conditions that trigger high N₂O emission events?

**SQ1（生物地球化学）：** 在何种土壤湿度、温度和碳可用性条件下，反硝化作用倾向于产生N₂O而非完全反硝化为N₂？我们能否识别触发高N₂O排放事件的阈值条件？

**SQ2 (Microbial):** How do fertilizer type (urea, ammonium nitrate, enhanced-efficiency) and application timing shape the community structure of ammonia oxidizers (AOA, AOB) and denitrifiers, and what is the functional significance of these community shifts for N₂O emissions?

**SQ2（微生物学）：** 肥料类型（尿素、硝酸铵、增效肥料）和施用时间如何塑造氨氧化菌（AOA、AOB）和反硝化菌的群落结构？这些群落变化对N₂O排放有何功能意义？

**SQ3 (Hydrological):** What is the temporal lag between fertilizer application and nitrate breakthrough in groundwater in different hydrogeological settings, and how does this lag complicate source attribution and mitigation?

**SQ3（水文学）：** 在不同水文地质条件下，施肥与地下水硝酸盐突破之间的时间滞后是多少？这种滞后如何复杂化来源识别和缓解措施？

**SQ4 (Integrated Management):** Can machine learning models trained on multi-year, multi-site datasets predict the site-specific optimal nitrogen rate that maximizes yield while minimizing combined N₂O + leaching losses? How do these "win-win" zones vary spatially and temporally?

**SQ4（综合管理）：** 基于多年、多地点数据集训练的机器学习模型能否预测特定地点的最佳氮施用量，以在最大化产量的同时最小化N₂O和淋溶的综合损失？这些"双赢"区域在时空上如何变化？

### 2.4 Methodology 研究方法论

**Research Paradigm:** Mixed-methods: Process-based biogeochemistry + Molecular microbial ecology + Computational modeling + On-farm participatory research

**研究范式：** 混合方法：基于过程的生物地球化学 + 分子微生物生态学 + 计算建模 + 农场参与式研究

**Phase 1: Intensive Field Monitoring (Years 1-2)**

| Measurement | Method | Frequency | Purpose |
|-------------|--------|-----------|---------|
| N₂O Fluxes | Static chambers + automated greenhouse gas analyzers | Daily to weekly | Quantify emissions; identify hot moments |
| Soil Inorganic N | KCl extractions | Weekly | Track ammonium/nitrate dynamics |
| Soil Moisture/Temperature | Continuous sensors | Hourly | Drivers of transformation |
| Microbial Communities | DNA/RNA sequencing (amplicon, metagenomics) | Seasonal | Functional potential vs. expression |
| Groundwater Nitrate | Monitoring wells | Monthly | Leaching loss quantification |
| Crop Yield | Harvest samples | Annual | Agronomic outcome |

**Experimental Design:**
- 4 sites × 3 replicates × 5 fertilizer treatments = 60 plots
- Treatments: control, urea, urea + nitrification inhibitor, split application, enhanced-efficiency polymer-coated urea
- Sites: different soil textures, drainage classes, climates

**Phase 2: Laboratory Incubation Experiments (Years 2-3)**

| Experiment | Conditions | Measurements | Purpose |
|------------|------------|--------------|---------|
| Temperature Gradient | 5-35°C | N₂O, CO₂, N₂ | Q₁₀ relationships for denitrification |
| Moisture Gradient | 40-100% WFPS | N₂O isotopomers | Distinguish fungal/bacterial pathways |
| Carbon Addition | Glucose gradients | Gene expression (qPCR) | C limitation of denitrification |
| Freeze-Thaw Cycles | Simulated winter/spring | N₂O pulses | Spring thaw emissions mechanism |

**Phase 3: Modeling & Integration (Years 3-4)**

| Model Type | Specific Model | Purpose | Integration |
|------------|----------------|---------|-------------|
| Process-based | DAYCENT, DNDC | Simulate N fate under scenarios | Calibrate with field data |
| Statistical | Machine learning (Random Forest, XGBoost) | Identify key predictors of N₂O hot moments | Train on multi-site data |
| Hydrological | HYDRUS-1D/2D | Simulate nitrate leaching | Link to vadose zone properties |
| Decision Support | Novel algorithm development | Farmer-facing tool | Synthesize findings for application |

**Phase 4: Participatory On-Farm Validation (Year 4)**
- Work with 20 farmers to test model-recommended precision N management
- Compare against their conventional practice
- Measure both yield and losses (simplified protocols)
- Socio-economic analysis of adoption barriers

### 2.5 Expected Contributions 预期贡献

**Theoretical Contributions:**
- Refinement of N₂O production partitioning models with in-situ microbial data
- Integration of hydrological lag times into nitrogen cascade framework
- Threshold identification for N₂O "hot moments"

**Empirical Contributions:**
- Multi-year, multi-site dataset linking microbial communities to N₂O fluxes
- First comprehensive N balance for under-studied cropping systems
- Isotopomer-based pathway partitioning under field conditions

**Practical Contributions:**
- Validated machine learning model for site-specific nitrogen recommendation
- Farmer-friendly decision support tool
- Policy-relevant quantification of N₂O mitigation potential

# DOMAIN 3: SPORTS SCIENCE & THE SOCIOLOGY OF THE FEMALE ATHLETE BODY
## 研究领域三：运动科学与女性运动员身体社会学

### Dissertation Title 论文标题
**"The Gendered Body in Performance: A Mixed-Methods Investigation of Body Image, Energy Availability, and Dual-Career Stress in Elite Female Athletes"**
**《表现中的性别化身体：精英女性运动员身体意象、能量可用性与双职业压力的混合方法研究》**

### 3.1 Research Problem & Justification 研究问题与论证

**Problem Statement:**
Elite female athletes occupy a unique and often contradictory position: their bodies are simultaneously instruments of performance, objects of aesthetic scrutiny, and sites of physiological stress. The convergence of intense training demands, the pressure to "juggle jobs or study with their sporting careers," and pervasive "body image concerns" creates a perfect storm for **Relative Energy Deficiency in Sport (REDs)** , with consequences for bone health, endocrine function, and mental well-being. Yet the lived experience of these pressures—from the "fairly morning" routines to the internal negotiation between athletic identity and social expectations—remains undertheorized and poorly addressed by sports institutions.

**研究陈述：**
精英女性运动员处于一个独特且常矛盾的位置：她们的身体同时是表现的工具、审美审视的对象和生理应激的场所。高强度训练需求、"平衡工作或学习与运动生涯"的压力以及普遍的"身体意象问题"的汇聚，为**运动相关性相对能量缺乏(REDs)**创造了完美风暴，对骨骼健康、内分泌功能和心理健康产生后果。然而这些压力的生活经验——从"相当早的早晨"例行公事到运动身份与社会期望之间的内部协商——仍未得到充分理论化，且运动机构对此处理不足。

**Research Gap:**
1. Disconnect between sports medicine (REDs physiology) and sports sociology (body image, identity)
2. Limited qualitative research on how female athletes *narrate* their body relationship across competitive cycle
3. Insufficient attention to dual-career stress as a distinct risk factor for REDs
4. Lack of intervention studies addressing both physiological and psychological dimensions
5. Under-theorization of how sport-specific uniform and aesthetic norms shape body image

### 3.3 Core Research Questions 核心研究问题

**Overarching Question:**
How do body image concerns, shaped by socio-cultural pressures, interact with the physiological demands of elite sport and the structural challenges of dual careers to influence energy availability, health outcomes, and performance in elite female athletes?

**总括性问题：**
由社会文化压力塑造的身体意象问题如何与精英运动的生理需求及双职业的结构性挑战相互作用，影响精英女性运动员的能量可用性、健康结果和表现？

**Sub-Questions:**

**SQ1 (Phenomenological):** How do elite female athletes across different sport types (aesthetic vs. non-aesthetic, weight-class vs. non-weight-class) narrate their relationship with their bodies? How does this narrative evolve across the competitive season and career trajectory?

**SQ1（现象学）：** 不同运动类型（审美型vs非审美型、体重分级vs非体重分级）的精英女性运动员如何叙述她们与身体的关系？这种叙述如何随赛季和职业生涯轨迹演变？

**SQ2 (Physiological):** What is the prevalence of subclinical and clinical REDs in a diverse sample of elite female athletes, and how accurately do commonly used screening tools (LEAF-Q, EDE-Q) predict objective measures of low energy availability (resting metabolic rate ratio, hormonal status)?

**SQ2（生理学）：** 在多样化的精英女性运动员样本中，亚临床和临床REDs的患病率是多少？常用筛查工具（LEAF-Q, EDE-Q）预测低能量可用性客观指标（静息代谢率比值、激素状态）的准确性如何？

**SQ3 (Dual-Career):** How does the stress of "juggling" athletic and academic/professional commitments moderate the relationship between body image concerns and disordered eating behaviors? Do dual-career athletes face additive risk?

**SQ3（双职业）：** "平衡"运动与学术/职业承诺的压力如何调节身体意象问题与进食障碍行为之间的关系？双职业运动员是否面临叠加风险？

**SQ4 (Temporal):** What does the "fairly morning" look like for athletes across different sports and support systems? How do the hidden labors of early mornings, multiple training sessions, and recovery activities accumulate to impact total energy expenditure and recovery opportunities?

**SQ4（时间性）：** 不同运动和支持系统的运动员的"相当早的早晨"是什么样的？清晨早起、多次训练和恢复活动的隐性劳动如何累积影响总能量消耗和恢复机会？

**SQ5 (Intervention):** Can a multi-component intervention targeting body image resilience, nutrition education, and dual-career support improve energy availability and reduce REDs risk compared to standard care?

**SQ5（干预）：** 与标准护理相比，针对身体意象韧性、营养教育和双职业支持的多组分干预能否改善能量可用性并降低REDs风险？

### 3.4 Methodology 研究方法论

**Research Paradigm:** Sequential explanatory mixed-methods: Quantitative physiological screening → Qualitative phenomenological exploration → Intervention development and testing

**研究范式：** 顺序解释性混合方法：定量生理筛查 → 定性现象学探索 → 干预开发与测试

**Phase 1: Large-Scale Screening (Year 1)**

| Measure | Instrument/Tool | Sample | Outcome |
|---------|-----------------|--------|---------|
| REDs Risk | LEAF-Q, EDE-Q, customized dual-career stress scale | N=500 elite female athletes (multiple sports, ages 18-35) | Prevalence data; risk factor identification |
| Body Image | Multidimensional Body-Self Relations Questionnaire | Same sample | Sport-specific body image profiles |
| Dual-Career Stress | Newly developed and validated scale | Same sample | Psychometric validation; descriptive data |
| Menstrual Function | Menstrual history questionnaire | Same sample | Prevalence of menstrual dysfunction |

**Phase 2: In-Depth Physiological & Qualitative Sub-Study (Years 1-2)**

*Sample:* Stratified purposive sample of n=60 from Phase 1 (high-risk, low-risk, across sport types)

| Domain | Measurements | Purpose |
|--------|--------------|---------|
| **Physiological** | RMR (indirect calorimetry), DXA scan (body composition, bone density), fasting hormones (estrogen, testosterone, cortisol, T3, leptin), 3-day weighed food records + accelerometry | Objective energy availability calculation; bone health assessment |
| **Qualitative** | 3x in-depth interviews (pre-season, mid-season, off-season); training observation; photo-elicitation (athletes photograph their bodies/food/training) | Lived experience; temporal dynamics; hidden labors |

# DOMAIN 4: MATERIAL SCIENCE & SELF-HEALING INFRASTRUCTURE
## 研究领域四：材料科学与自愈合基础设施

### Dissertation Title 论文标题
**"Living Infrastructure: Towards Autonomous Healing and Sensing in Cementitious Materials"**
**《活的 infrastructure：迈向水泥基材料的自主愈合与感知》**

### 4.1 Research Problem & Justification 研究问题与论证

**Problem Statement:**
Conventional infrastructure is fundamentally passive and static. When cracks appear—"potholes and cracks in bridge"—they propagate, allowing water and chlorides to penetrate, leading to reinforcement corrosion and eventual structural failure. Current maintenance is reactive, costly, and disruptive. Drawing inspiration from biological systems "teeming with life," this dissertation explores a paradigm shift: creating materials that "repair themselves, heal themselves, actuate, sense the environment." The challenge lies in engineering multifunctionality without compromising primary structural performance.

**研究陈述：**
传统基础设施本质上是被动和静态的。当裂缝出现——"坑洼和桥梁裂缝"——它们会扩展，允许水和氯化物渗透，导致钢筋腐蚀并最终结构失效。当前的维护是反应性的、昂贵的且具有破坏性。从"充满生命"的生物系统汲取灵感，本论文探索一种范式转变：创造能够"自我修复、自愈合、驱动、感知环境"的材料。挑战在于在不影响主要结构性能的前提下工程化多功能性。

**Research Gap:**
1. Trade-off between healing efficiency and mechanical strength remains unresolved
2. Scalability of lab-scale healing technologies to field applications unproven
3. Integration of sensing and healing functions in a single system rarely attempted
4. Long-term durability of healing agents (bacteria, polymers) unknown
5. Life-cycle assessment comparing self-healing vs. conventional maintenance lacking

### 4.3 Core Research Questions 核心研究问题

**Overarching Question:**
Can we design a cementitious composite that simultaneously achieves high structural performance, autonomous crack healing across multiple damage events, and embedded sensing capabilities, and can such a material be scaled from laboratory to field applications with favorable life-cycle costs?

**总括性问题：**
我们能否设计一种水泥基复合材料，同时实现高结构性能、跨多次损伤事件的自主动裂缝愈合以及嵌入式感知能力？这种材料能否从实验室扩展到现场应用，并具有有利的生命周期成本？

**Sub-Questions:**

**SQ1 (Bacterial Healing):** Which spore-forming alkaliphilic bacterial strains (e.g., *Bacillus* species) exhibit the highest ureolytic activity and calcium carbonate precipitation efficiency in the high-pH, low-nutrient environment of hardened concrete? How can bacterial spores be protected (encapsulation) to survive concrete mixing and remain viable for years?

**SQ1（细菌愈合）：** 在硬化混凝土的高pH、低营养环境中，哪些形成孢子的嗜碱细菌菌株（如*芽孢杆菌*属）表现出最高的脲酶活性和碳酸钙沉淀效率？如何保护（封装）细菌孢子以在混凝土搅拌中存活并保持多年活性？

**SQ2 (Mechanical Compatibility):** What is the optimal size, concentration, and shell material for polymeric microcapsules containing healing agents (e.g., cyanoacrylates, epoxies) to ensure capsule rupture upon crack propagation without acting as stress concentrators that reduce pre-crack strength?

**SQ2（机械兼容性）：** 含愈合剂（如氰基丙烯酸酯、环氧树脂）的聚合物微胶囊的最佳尺寸、浓度和壳材料是什么，以确保在裂缝扩展时胶囊破裂，同时不充当降低预裂强度的应力集中物？

**SQ3 (Sensing Integration):** Can a percolating network of conductive fillers (carbon nanotubes, graphene nanoplatelets, steel microfibers) be designed to create a strain-sensitive composite where changes in electrical resistance (piezoresistivity) correlate with crack width and location, effectively creating a material that can "sense the environment"?

**SQ3（感知集成）：** 能否设计导电填料（碳纳米管、石墨烯纳米片、钢微纤维）的渗流网络，创建应变敏感复合材料，其中电阻变化（压阻效应）与裂缝宽度和位置相关，有效创造能够"感知环境"的材料？

**SQ4 (Multifunctional Synergy):** How do bacterial healing agents and conductive sensing networks interact? Does bacterial metabolic activity affect long-term conductivity? Does the presence of conductive fillers affect bacterial viability or healing efficiency?

**SQ4（多功能协同）：** 细菌愈合剂和导电感知网络如何相互作用？细菌代谢活动是否影响长期导电性？导电填料的存在是否影响细菌活性或愈合效率？

**SQ5 (Life-Cycle Assessment):** What is the full life-cycle cost and environmental impact of self-healing concrete compared to conventional concrete with standard maintenance and repair regimes over a 50-100 year design life?

**SQ5（生命周期评估）：** 与常规混凝土及标准维护和修复方案相比，自愈合混凝土在50-100年设计寿命内的完整生命周期成本和环境影响如何？

**研究范式：** 材料科学实验设计 + 多物理场建模 + 生命周期评估
