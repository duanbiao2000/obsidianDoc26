## 一、脑图（```mindmap```格式）

```mindmap
## **Hack 7: Do an Evening Reset**
- Reflect on today: record what went well & what was learned
- Review goals three times a day to hit targets
- Plan tomorrow: check calendar, tweak plans & inform assistant
## **Core Concepts**
- Consistency compounds
- Downtime is part of the creative process
- Winners are made in the evenings
## **Tools & References**
- 5-minute journal (for evening reset)
- Eisenhower Matrix (for priority sorting)
- Book: Never Eat Alone (Keith Farazzi)
- Book: Buy Back Your Time (Dan Martell)
```
