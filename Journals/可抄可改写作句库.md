先给你一批「可抄可改写作句库」，都来自这个 masterclass 的高质量句型，每条给 3 个改写/应用例句，方便你在雅思写作和口语里套用。​

---

## 1. Fluency is not about knowing more English. It is about thinking in English.

核心功能：颠覆直觉 + 下定义，适合引言段或转折句。

- 原句：Fluency is not about knowing more English. Fluency is about thinking in English.​

例句组合：

1. In my view, academic success is not about spending more hours in class; it is about thinking more clearly about what you learn.
2. Effective communication is not about using complicated vocabulary; it is about expressing complex ideas in simple, precise language.
3. True digital literacy is not about knowing more apps; it is about thinking critically about how technology shapes your life.

---

## 2. The translation habit you have developed is not a failure. It is your brain doing exactly what it was designed to do.

功能：先安抚、再解释本质；可用来写“看似缺点其实是系统选择”。

- 原句：The translation habit you have developed is not a failure. It is actually your brain doing exactly what it was designed to do.​

例句：

1. Procrastination is not pure laziness; it is your brain trying to avoid short‑term discomfort, even at the cost of long‑term goals.
2. Exam anxiety is not a personal weakness; it is your nervous system responding to a situation it perceives as a threat.
3. Social media addiction is not just a lack of willpower; it is your brain reacting exactly as it was designed to when exposed to endless instant rewards.

---

## 3. Imagine you are trying to run a race, but before every single stride, you have to stop and tie your shoe.

功能：类比句，解释抽象问题造成的效率损失。

- 原句：Imagine you are trying to run a race, but before every single stride, you have to stop and tie your shoe. That is what translation does to your speech.​

例句：

1. Imagine you are trying to write an essay, but every few words you stop to check your phone. That is what constant multitasking does to your concentration.
2. Imagine a city where every traffic light turns red for a full minute whenever one car passes. That is what unnecessary bureaucracy does to economic growth.
3. Imagine you are learning to swim, but every time you move your arms, someone pulls you back to the edge. That is what excessive fear of mistakes does to language learners.

---

## 4. Fluency does not live in your analytical mind. It lives in your automatic mind.

功能：对立结构 A…not…B，用于强调真正所在位置。

- 原句：Fluency does not live in your analytical mind. It lives in your automatic mind.​

例句：

1. Confidence does not live in your public image. It lives in your private thoughts about yourself.
2. Creativity does not live in rare moments of inspiration. It lives in daily routines that allow ideas to appear.
3. Real learning does not live in test scores. It lives in the habits you keep when nobody is grading you.

---

## 5. The secret of immersion is not geography. The secret of immersion is pressure, purpose, and repetition.

功能：It’s not X, it’s YZ 结构；非常适合反驳表面原因。​

- 原句：The secret of immersion is not geography. The secret of immersion is pressure, purpose, and repetition in the target language.​

例句：

1. The secret of effective online education is not technology. The secret is clear structure, human connection, and consistent feedback.
2. The secret of a healthy lifestyle is not expensive equipment. The secret is small daily choices that you can repeat for years.
3. The secret of becoming confident in public speaking is not talent. The secret is preparation, exposure, and the courage to stand up again after a bad speech.

---

## 6. Everything changes when you start training in English from English.

功能：强调临界点 / 转折点；常用于提出关键做法。​

- 原句：Everything changes when you start training in English from English.​

例句：

1. Everything changes when you stop studying for the test and start studying for your life.
2. Everything changes when you measure progress by habits, not by motivation.
3. Everything changes when you stop asking “Is this easy?” and start asking “Is this worth it?”

---

## 7. Words that stick are words that you have encountered in meaningful contexts.

功能：定义原则型句子，可放 body 段开头。​

- 原句：Words that stick are words that you have encountered in meaningful contexts.​

例句：

1. Skills that last are skills that you have applied to real problems, not just practiced in exercises.
2. Relationships that endure are relationships that have survived conflict, not just enjoyed good times.
3. Values that guide you are values that you have tested in difficult situations, not just written on paper.

---

## 8. Your inner monologue is the rehearsal space for your spoken language.

功能：隐喻句，X is the Y for Z；适合写“X 的真正作用”。​

- 原句：Your inner monologue is the rehearsal space for your spoken language.​

例句：

1. Social media is the stage where many teenagers rehearse their identity.
2. The classroom is the safest laboratory for students to test their ideas and make intellectual mistakes.
3. University life is the training ground for how young adults will manage freedom and responsibility.

---

## 9. The goal is to make English the water you swim in, not a subject you study.

功能：比喻 + not A but B；非常适合写「理想状态」。​

- 原句：The goal of everything in this chapter is to make English the water you swim in, not a subject you study.​

例句：

1. The goal of education should be to make curiosity the air students breathe, not a homework task they complete.
2. The goal of environmental policy is to make sustainability the default, not a rare personal choice.
3. The goal of digital literacy is to make critical thinking the habit you live with, not a skill you use only in exams.

---

## 10. Fluency is not something that happens to you. It is something you build brick by brick, day by day.

功能：结尾金句，用于强调“主动性 + 过程感”。​

- 原句：Fluency is not something that happens to you. It is something you build brick by brick, day by day.​

例句：

1. Confidence is not something you are born with. It is something you construct through repeated small risks.
2. A strong society is not something that appears overnight. It is something citizens create through daily choices and shared responsibilities.
3. A meaningful life is not something you discover by accident. It is something you design through countless ordinary days.

## 一、反驳/纠正直觉型（Not A, but B）

**核心模板 1：X is not about A. It is about B.**

- Fluency is not about knowing more English. It is about thinking in English.​

用法：用在引言或首段，反驳题目中的简单想法。

- Education is not about passing exams. It is about learning to think and solve real problems.

**核心模板 2：The secret of X is not A. The secret is B, C and D.**

- The secret of immersion is not geography. The secret of immersion is pressure, purpose, and repetition.​

用法：用在 body 段，指出真正关键因素。

- The secret of effective online learning is not technology. The secret is structure, feedback and self‑discipline.

---

## 二、安抚+本质解释型（貌似失败，其实是机制）

**模板：X is not a failure / weakness. It is your brain / system doing what it was designed to do.**

- The translation habit you have developed is not a failure. It is your brain doing exactly what it was designed to do.​

用法：适合分析“焦虑、拖延、成瘾”等现象，显得理解人性。

- Exam anxiety is not a personal weakness. It is your body doing what it was designed to do when it senses danger.

---

## 三、比喻说明型（Imagine… That is what X does）

**模板：Imagine… That is what X does to Y.**

- Imagine you are trying to run a race, but before every single stride, you have to stop and tie your shoe. That is what translation does to your speech.​

用法：解释抽象问题（翻译、拖延、官僚主义）的“效率杀伤力”。

- Imagine you are trying to write an essay, but every few minutes someone shouts your name. That is what constant phone notifications do to your concentration.

---

## 四、真正所在/归属型（does not live in A, it lives in B）

**模板：X does not live in A. It lives in B.**

- Fluency does not live in your analytical mind. It lives in your automatic mind.​

用法：强调某种能力的“真实来源”，适合 body 段 topic sentence。

- Confidence does not live in certificates. It lives in the experiences that prove you can handle difficulties.

---

## 五、转折点/临界变化型（Everything changes when…）

**模板：Everything changes when you start / stop doing X.**

- Everything changes when you start training in English from English.​

用法：引出关键行动建议或“转折经验”。

- Everything changes when you stop studying only for exams and start studying for your own life.

---

## 六、原则/定义型（X that Y are X that…）

**模板：Xs that last / stick are Xs that…**

- Words that stick are words that you have encountered in meaningful contexts.​

用法：定义「什么样的东西是有效的」。

- Skills that last are skills that you use to solve real problems, not just practice in exercises.

---

## 七、隐喻定位型（X is the Y for Z）

**模板：X is the Y for Z.**

- Your inner monologue is the rehearsal space for your spoken language.​

用法：一句话把抽象概念变得可视化，很适合开头或强调句。

- School is the training ground for how young people will handle freedom and responsibility.

---

## 八、目标状态型（make X the water/air, not a subject）

**模板：The goal is to make X the Y you live in, not a subject you study.**

- The goal is to make English the water you swim in, not a subject you study.​

用法：写结尾段，描述理想目标。

- The goal of education should be to make curiosity the air students breathe, not a task they complete for homework.

---

## 九、过程构建型结尾（not something that happens to you）

**模板：X is not something that happens to you. It is something you build…**

- Fluency is not something that happens to you. It is something you build brick by brick, day by day.​

用法：做全文最后一句，很有「收束感」。

- A meaningful career is not something that happens to you. It is something you create through daily choices over many years.

## 一、时间与习惯描述（日常情境模板）

功能：Part 1/2 描述习惯、经历；写作里讲 routine / lifestyle。

- I prefer to work in the morning when it is quiet.​
- The meeting starts exactly at 9 a.m. every Tuesday.​
- The team worked through the night to meet the launch date.​
- The new café on the corner serves excellent coffee.​

用途：

- 直接用于口语 Part 1（work/study, daily routine）；
- 写作里可改成第三人称或泛指。

---

## 二、决定与改变（decision / change）

功能：讲“做出选择、职业转变、后悔、困难决定”。

- I realised I was no longer growing in my old job.​
- Choosing between two job offers was extremely difficult.​
- What would you have done differently in that situation?​
- He tends to overthink things before making any decision.​

用途：

- Task 2 职业/人生选择题；
- Part 2: _Describe a difficult decision you made_。

---

## 三、观点与评价（opinion / evaluation）

功能：表达看法，带一点 nuance，非常雅思 Part 3 风格。

- It depends a lot on the type of task involved.​
- I think it suits some personalities, but not everyone.​
- Sometimes, but first impressions can also be quite misleading.​
- The documentary significantly changed their perspective.​

用途：

- 任何问 “Do you think…?” 的题，都可以用这些开头。

---

## 四、原因与结果（cause & effect）

功能：讲理由、结果、影响；Task 2 body 段通用。

- The project took longer than expected due to unforeseen issues.​
- The outdoor event was cancelled because of strong wind warnings.​
- The findings suggest a strong correlation between sleep and concentration.​
- The policy was implemented with little regard for its side effects.​

用途：

- 环境、健康、工作效率类作文都可直接套。

---

## 五、习惯与生产力（habit / productivity）

功能：讲 self‑improvement、效率、routine。

- Absolutely. Routines give your day structure and purpose.​
- Finishing each day by planning the first hour of the next has improved my productivity.​
- The value of small, consistent daily effort over time is often overlooked.​
- The habit of reflection is what separates reactive action from intentional action.​

用途：

- Task 2 关于「self‑discipline / success」；
- 口语 Part 3 讨论 productivity / habits。

---

## 六、学习与技能发展（learning / skill）

功能：讲学习方法、终身学习、再教育。

- Wide reading and regular writing make a real difference to your vocabulary.​
- It can be harder to learn a language as an adult, but adults have real advantages in motivation.​
- The most valuable thing a learner can do every day is to listen to the language as it is really used, with full presence.​
- The journey towards fluency is not a straight line, but a long looping spiral.​

用途：

- Task 2 教育/语言学习题；
- 也可以放进雅思口语“how to improve English”。

---

## 七、批判性思维与判断（critical thinking）

功能：作文里显得「思维在线」的句子。

- Perfectionism can significantly slow down progress.​
- It is essential; without honest feedback, growth is much slower.​
- Sometimes hurrying rarely produces the best results.​
- He was careful not to draw premature conclusions.​

用途：

- Task 2 关于 decision‑making / education / work；
- Part 3 常见：mistakes / feedback / leadership。

---

## 八、关系与沟通（communication / relationships）

功能：表现“情商、沟通、合作”。

- What separates good communicators from great ones is the ability to make complex ideas feel simple and accessible.​
- The gap between intention and impact is where most communication fails.​
- The most effective feedback comes as recognition, not as judgement.​
- The richest conversations are the ones that leave you different from how they found you.​

用途：

- Task 2 关于 communication / social media / workplace；
- 口语 Part 3 讨论 communication skills / leadership。

---

## 九、语言与思维（language & thought）——高端哲学味

功能：C1 气质，适合作文结尾或高分口语收束。

- Language is not just a tool of communication. It is a medium of thought.​
- The quality of our thinking is inseparable from the quality of our language.​
- Some of what is most true in us will never fully fit into words.​
- Fluency is always approximate, and that incompleteness is what keeps the language alive.​

用途：

- Task 2 关于语言、教育、communication 的收尾金句；
- Part 3 高分 candidate 展示“抽象话题处理能力”。

## 1）表达“逐步、渐进变化”（gradually / slowly）

**功能：描述进步、趋势，Task 2 / Part 3 通用。**

- I’m gradually becoming more comfortable speaking English.
    
- I’m gradually adjusting my routine to make more time for English practice.
    
- My improvement has been gradual but consistent over the past few months.
    

---

## 2）表达“努力与过程”（I’m working on / I’m in the process of）

**功能：说明你在持续改进，既谦虚又积极。**

- I’m working on speaking with more clarity and confidence.
    
- I’m in the process of refining my English speaking skills.
    
- I’m working on balancing my work and study schedule more efficiently.
    

---

## 3）表达“略懂 / 不完全懂”（to some extent / to a certain degree）

**功能：给出「部分同意 / 部分理解」，非常地道。**

- I understand this concept to some extent, but I still need more practice.
    
- To a certain degree, I agree that technology makes life easier.
    
- People do benefit from social media to some extent, but there are clear risks as well.
    

---

## 4）表达“有点被压垮 / 勉强应付”（overwhelming / manageable / caught me off guard）

**功能：描述压力和应对，讲经历特别好用。**

- Sometimes the amount of information feels overwhelming, but I’m trying to stay focused.
    
- I want to make my learning routine more manageable and realistic.
    
- That question caught me off guard, but I managed to give a clear answer.
    

---

## 5）表达“坚持、一点点积累”（consistency / small steps / makes a huge difference）

**功能：谈学习/成长，写结论段很合适。**

- Practicing English every day, even for a few minutes, makes a huge difference.
    
- Small, consistent daily effort often leads to significant progress over time.
    
- Staying consistent is the most efficient way to make noticeable improvement.
    

---

## 6）表达“意识、觉察、反思”（I’ve been reflecting / I’m starting to realize）

**功能：显示思考深度，适合高分作文和 Part 3。**

- I’ve been reflecting on my learning habits and how they affect my progress.
    
- I’m starting to realize that fluency comes more from use than from theory.
    
- I’ve been reflecting on the role of technology in our daily lives.
    

---

## 7）表达“态度：开放、谦虚、有礼貌”（I’m open to / I’d appreciate it if / I see where you’re coming from）

**功能：讨论 / 辩论 / 题目观点时显得成熟。**

- I’m open to suggestions on how I can improve my speaking.
    
- I’d appreciate it if you could clarify that point again.
    
- I see where you’re coming from, but I have a slightly different perspective.
    

---

## 8）表达“逐渐清晰 & 证据感”（it’s becoming clear / from what I can tell）

**功能：做推论、总结观点。**

- It’s becoming clear that daily practice is essential for real progress.
    
- From what I can tell, my listening skills have improved a lot recently.
    
- It’s becoming clear that many people feel overwhelmed by constant notifications.
    

---

## 9）表达“目标与方向”（what I’m aiming for / I’m trying to prioritize）

**功能：讲目标、规划、未来。**

- What I’m aiming for is natural, confident communication in English.
    
- I’m trying to prioritize my English practice without neglecting other responsibilities.
    
- What I’m aiming for is a routine that I can actually stick to long term.
    

---

## 10）表达“自信与自我评价”（I’m getting the hang of / I’m starting to feel more at ease）

**功能：描述自信提升，适合故事结尾或反思。**

- I’m getting the hang of expressing my ideas more clearly.
    
- I’m starting to feel more at ease when I speak with native speakers.
    
- I can feel myself becoming more confident every time I practice.
