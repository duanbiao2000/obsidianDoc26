## 时间与价值

- decide what to do with the time that is given to us（决定如何使用被赋予的时间）​
- time is the most valuable currency（时间是最有价值的“货币”）​
- time is not just something you spend. It's something you decide.（时间不是被“花掉”的，而是被“决定怎么用”的）​
- time is exponentially more powerful than money（时间比金钱强大得多）​
- time is nonrenewable / time is not renewable（时间不可再生）​
- once you spend it, it's gone（时间一旦花掉就没了）​

## 错误观念与反驳

- the most common mistake people make（人们最常犯的错误）​
- we've been made to believe this fallacy that time is money（我们被灌输“时间就是金钱”这个错误观念）​
- trade time for money（用时间换钱）​

## 目标与“不可思议目标”

- be tasked with the impossible goal（被赋予一个不可能完成的目标）​
- set your impossible goal（设定你的“不可能目标”）​
- a bigger and better reality / a bigger and better goal（更大更好的现实 / 目标）​
- 10X better, not just 2X（提升十倍，而不是只是两倍）​
- set an aggressive timeline of three years or less（给目标设定三年或更短的激进时间线）​

## 时间作为工具

- time is a tool（时间是一种工具）​
- harness the power of time（驾驭时间的力量）​
- use time as a tool to achieve your goals（把时间当工具来实现目标）​
- buy someone more time（为某人“争取更多时间”）​
- add to our time / slow time down（延长、放慢我们主观上的时间）​

## 决策与行动

- all we have to decide is what to do with the time…（我们唯一要决定的，就是怎么用时间）​
- start making different decisions in the present（开始在当下做出不同的决定）​
- take actions to buy more time（采取行动来争取时间）​
- be forced into an impossible goal（被迫进入一个不可能的目标）​

## 路径与期限

- come up with a one-year version and a 90-day version of this goal（把大目标拆成一年版和 90 天版）​
- depending on the time frame, you're creating different pathways（根据时间框架，会产生不同解决路径）​
- if I had to do it in 90 days, what do I need to do?（如果必须在 90 天内做到，该怎么做？）​

## 叙事与隐喻

- the stakes are high / the stakes continuously escalating（赌注很高 / 风险和压力不断升级）​
- a looming deadline（逼近的最后期限）​
- go after time, not riches（追求时间，而不是财富）​
