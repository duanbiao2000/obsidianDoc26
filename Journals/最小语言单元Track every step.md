# 最小语言单元-地道自然词汇块提取

我从这段**多风格车祸描述**原文中，提取**高频、实用、可直接套用**的词汇块（lexical chunks），按**使用场景**分类，附**原文例句**，方便直接背诵和使用：

# 一、日常口语 / 非正式书信（给朋友、聊天）
1. **out of nowhere**
   突然；不知从哪儿冒出来
   例句：when out of nowhere This SUV came barreling through the intersection and hit my car.

2. **be totaled (AmE)**
   （车辆）报废
   例句：before I knew it my car was totaled.

3. **get checked out**
   （去医院）做检查
   例句：I had to go to the hospital just to get checked out.

4. **be at fault**
   有过错；负全责
   例句：the other driver was definitely at fault.

5. **exchange information**
   交换（保险/联系）信息
   例句：we exchanged information and everything.

6. **stay alert**
   保持警惕
   例句：it's just a reminder to always stay alert and be careful out there.

# 二、文学描写（写作、叙事）
1. **shatter the serenity**
   打破宁静
   例句：screeching tires and the shattering of glass shattered the serenity of the afternoon.

2. **be heavy with**
   弥漫着；充满
   例句：the air was Heavy with the acrid smell of burnt rubber and gasoline.

3. **extricate sb from**
   把某人从……中救出
   例句：trying to extricate the occupants from the Twisted Metal.

4. **leave an indelible mark**
   留下不可磨灭的印记
   例句：the aftermath of the accident left an indelible mark on those who witnessed it.

5. **in the blink of an eye**
   转眼间；一瞬间
   例句：in the blink of an eye everything can change.
