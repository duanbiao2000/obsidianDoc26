## **二、易混发音&易错词（听力/口语必坑）**
  - 长短音混淆
    - ship /ʃɪp/ vs sheep /ʃiːp/
      例句：Can you hear the difference between ship and sheep?
    - work /wɜːk/ vs walk /wɔːk/
      例句：Can you hear the difference between work and walk?
  - 元音/重音易错
    - angry /ˈæŋɡri/ vs hungry /ˈhʌŋɡri/
      例句：Can you hear the difference between angry and hungry?
    - desert /ˈdezət/ 沙漠 vs dessert /dɪˈzɜːt/ 甜点
      例句：Can you hear the difference between desert and dessert?
  - 发音/拼写易错
    - muscle /ˈmʌsl/（不读k音）
      例句：Let me check the word muscle.
    - breath（名 /breθ/）vs breathe（动 /briːð/）
      例句：My breathing was out of control.
## **三、高频地道动词短语**
  - 情绪心理类
    - freak out 崩溃/恐慌
      例句：My mind freaked out quite seriously.
    - calm down 平静
      例句：They gave me medication to calm me down.
    - back sb up 支持/包庇
      例句：All the doctors were backing one another up.
  - 动作经历类
    - come up with 想出
      例句：My wife came up with a new idea.
    - make a difference 起作用
      例句：This idea really made the difference.
    - turn out fine 结果变好
      例句：Things turn out fine in the end.
  - 医疗场景类
    - have surgery 做手术
      例句：I got a surgery to cure my apnea.
    - knock sb out 使昏迷
      例句：The anesthesia knocked him out.
