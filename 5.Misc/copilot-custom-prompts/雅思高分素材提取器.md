### 🔷 动词短语 & 固定搭配（5–8个）

**选词原则：优先 B2 词汇 + 高级搭配，拒绝生僻词堆砌。**

|表达|释义|发音陷阱|例句|
|---|---|---|---|
|drive up the cost of|推高……成本|—|_Soaring demand has **driven up the cost of** housing in most major cities._|
|be shaped by|被……塑造/影响|—|_Our values are largely **shaped by** the environment we grow up in._|
|put pressure on|向……施压|—|_An ageing population is **putting pressure on** public healthcare systems worldwide._|

---

### 🔷 高分 vs 低分表达对比（4–6组）

**直击中国考生最常见的"低分替换"习惯。**

|❌ 低分写法|✅ 高分替换|说明|
|---|---|---|
|_is very important_|_**plays a vital role in**_|动词短语更有力|
|_has a bad effect on_|_**takes a toll on**_|简单词组合，地道自然|
|_because of_|_**in the wake of / owing to**_|逻辑关系更精准|
|_I think_|_**There is a growing recognition that**_|学术语气，规避第一人称|
