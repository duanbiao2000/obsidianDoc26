**雅思口语 Part 1 — 5-6分 vs 7-9分 对比示例（10组）**

---

**Topic 1: Do you like cooking?**

5-6分： Yes, I like cooking. I cook every day. I think cooking is interesting and healthy. I can make Chinese food.

7-9分： Honestly, it depends on my mood. When I have time, I genuinely enjoy experimenting with recipes — there's something satisfying about pulling off a dish you've never tried before. But on weekdays when I'm exhausted, it feels more like a chore than anything else.

---

**Topic 2: Do you use public transport?**

5-6分： Yes, I use subway every day. It is cheap and fast. Sometimes it is very crowded, I don't like that. But I still use it because it is convenient.

7-9分： Pretty regularly, yeah. I take the metro to work most mornings — it's just far more predictable than driving, especially with the traffic in my city. The only downside is rush hour; it gets unbearably packed, so I've started leaving about twenty minutes earlier to dodge that.

---

**Topic 3: Do you like reading?**

5-6分： Yes, I like reading very much. I read books in my free time. I like novels and sometimes magazines. Reading can make me relax and learn more knowledge.

7-9分： I used to read a lot more than I do now, to be honest. These days I tend to gravitate towards non-fiction — mostly books on psychology or history. I find fiction harder to commit to unless it really grabs me in the first chapter.

---

**Topic 4: What kind of weather do you like?**

5-6分： I like sunny weather because it is warm and I can go outside. I don't like rainy weather because it is inconvenient. In my city the weather is sometimes very hot in summer.

7-9分： I'm a big fan of that crisp, slightly cool weather you get in autumn — the kind where you can wear a light jacket and still feel comfortable walking outside for hours. Summer heat honestly drains me, so I tend to stay indoors a lot from June onwards.

---

**Topic 5: Do you enjoy shopping?**

5-6分： Sometimes I like shopping, sometimes I don't. I go shopping with my friends on weekends. I like buying clothes and shoes. Online shopping is also very convenient now.

7-9分： Not particularly, if I'm being honest. I find brick-and-mortar shopping a bit overwhelming — too many choices, too many people. I've basically shifted everything online at this point, which suits me fine because I can just filter exactly what I want without the whole experience.

---

**Topic 6: Do you like to take photos?**

5-6分： Yes, I take photos with my phone. I like to take photos when I travel. I also take food photos. I post them on social media to share with friends.

7-9分： I go through phases with it. When I'm travelling somewhere new, I'm quite snap-happy — I want to capture everything. But in day-to-day life, I've actually become more conscious about just being present rather than documenting every moment. I think social media kind of trained us to photograph more than we need to.

---

**Topic 7: Do you prefer mornings or evenings?**

5-6分： I prefer evenings because I can relax after work. In the morning I am tired and I need to wake up early. In the evening I can watch TV and use my phone freely.

7-9分： Evenings, without question. I've tried to become a morning person multiple times and it just never sticks — I do my best thinking after about 8pm. That's usually when I read, write, or work on anything that requires real concentration.

---

**Topic 8: Do you like learning new things?**

5-6分： Yes, I like learning new things. Learning is important for everyone. I learn many things in school and also from internet. I think learning makes people more clever.

7-9分： Very much so, though I've become more selective about what I invest time in. I'd rather go deep on one or two things than skim the surface of everything. Right now I'm fairly obsessed with learning about behavioural economics — it keeps reshaping how I interpret everyday decisions.

---

**Topic 9: How often do you watch TV?**

5-6分： I watch TV sometimes, maybe two or three times a week. I like watching TV shows and movies. My favourite is American TV series. I watch it to practice English and also for entertainment.

7-9分： Barely at all in the traditional sense — I don't even own a TV. But I stream things on my laptop fairly regularly, maybe a few hours a week. I tend to binge something on weekends and then not watch anything for a couple of weeks. It's pretty inconsistent.

---

**Topic 10: Do you like your hometown?**

5-6分： Yes, I like my hometown because my family is there. My hometown is a small city. The environment is good and food is delicious. But there are not many job opportunities so I came to the big city.

7-9分： It's grown on me, honestly. When I was younger I was desperate to leave — it felt too small and too slow. But whenever I go back now I really appreciate how unhurried everything is. There's something genuinely restorative about a place where nobody's in a rush.

---

**核心差异总结**

| 维度  | 5-6分            | 7-9分       |
| --- | --------------- | ---------- |
| 词汇  | 高频简单词，重复        | 自然搭配，有个人色彩 |
| 结构  | 短句堆叠            | 有层次，有转折    |
| 内容  | 泛泛而谈            | 有具体细节、个人立场 |
| 连接词 | and/but/because | 语义更丰富的衔接   |
| 真实感 | 像在背答案           | 像在真实交谈     |
