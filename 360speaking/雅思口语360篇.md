# 雅思口语 360 个话题

雅思口语分三个部分：Part 1（日常问答）、Part 2（独白描述）、Part 3（深度讨论）。

---

## Part 1 话题（日常生活类）共120题

Part 1 考官问简短问题，考生回答 2—3 句，话题围绕个人日常生活。

### 个人与家庭

1. [[Do you work or are you a student]]?
2. [[What do you do in your free time]]?
3. [[Where are you from originally]]?
4. [[Do you live in a house or an apartment]]?
5. [[What do you like about your hometown]]?
6. What would you change about your hometown?
7. Do you have a large or small family?
8. How often do you spend time with your family?
9. Did you enjoy your childhood?
10. What was your favourite subject at school?
11. Do you have a best friend? How did you meet?
12. Are you still in touch with friends from your childhood?
13. Do you prefer spending time alone or with others?
14. How do you usually celebrate your birthday?
15. Do you have any siblings? Are you close to them?

### 居住环境

1. Do you like the area where you currently live?
2. [[What kind of neighbourhood do you live in]]?
3. How long have you lived in your current home?
4. Would you prefer to live in the city or the countryside?
5. Is your home quiet or noisy?
6. What do you like most about your home?
7. [[Do you think you will move in the future]]?
8. How important is it to have a comfortable home?
9. Do you know your neighbours well?
10. What do you think makes a good neighbourhood?

### 工作与学习

1. What do you study and why did you choose that subject?
2. [[What do you enjoy most about your studies]]?
3. What are your career goals?
4. Do you prefer studying alone or in groups?
5. [[What skills do you think are most important for your future career]]?
6. Do you think your education has prepared you well for work?
7. What was the most useful thing you learned at school?
8. Do you enjoy the work you do?
9. [[What do you find most challenging about your job or studies]]?
10. Would you like to change your career in the future?

### 日常习惯

1. [[What time do you usually wake up]]?
2. Do you prefer mornings or evenings?
3. What do you usually eat for breakfast?
4. How do you usually get to work or school?
5. Do you prefer cooking at home or eating out?
6. How much time do you spend on your phone each day?
7. [[Do you have a daily exercise routine]]?
8. [[How do you usually wind down at the end of the day]]?
9. Do you prefer a busy or a relaxed daily routine?
10. [[How do you organise your time]]?

### 兴趣爱好

1. [[What hobbies do you have]]?
2. How did you develop your interest in your hobby?
3. Do you prefer indoor or outdoor activities?
4. Have your hobbies changed since you were a child?
5. [[Do you think it is important to have hobbies]]?
6. [[Do you enjoy reading]]? What kind of books do you like?
7. Do you enjoy listening to music? What genres do you prefer?
8. Do you play any musical instruments?
9. Do you enjoy watching films? What kinds do you prefer?
10. Do you enjoy cooking? What dishes can you make?

### 食物与饮食

1. [[What is your favourite food]]?
2. Do you prefer home-cooked meals or restaurant food?
3. Have your eating habits changed in recent years?
4. Do you enjoy trying food from other countries?
5. Is there any food you dislike? Why?
6. How often do you eat out?
7. Do you think the food in your country is healthy?
8. [[Are you interested in learning to cook new dishes]]?
9. [[Do you think diet and health are closely connected]]?
10. What is a traditional dish from your country?

### 旅行与假期

1. [[Do you enjoy travelling]]?
2. Where have you travelled to?
3. What kind of holidays do you prefer?
4. Do you prefer travelling alone or with others?
5. [[What do you usually do on public holidays]]?
6. Have you ever had a memorable travel experience?
7. Do you prefer beach holidays or city breaks?
8. How do you usually plan your holidays?
9. [[Would you like to live in another country]]?
10. What country would you most like to visit and why?

### 购物与消费

1. Do you enjoy shopping?
2. Do you prefer shopping online or in stores?
3. How often do you go shopping?
4. Do you think you are a careful spender?
5. What was the last thing you bought that you were really pleased with?
6. Do you ever regret purchases?
7. [[Do you prefer branded or unbranded products]]?
8. Do you enjoy window shopping?
9. How has online shopping changed your habits?
10. Do you think people today buy too many things they do not need?

### 科技与媒体

1. How often do you use the internet?
2. What do you mainly use your smartphone for?
3. Do you use social media? Which platforms do you use?
4. How do you usually get your news?
5. Do you think technology has improved your life?
6. Do you watch television? What kinds of programmes do you enjoy?
7. Do you prefer watching films at home or at the cinema?
8. Have you ever used video calling to keep in touch with people?
9. Do you think people spend too much time on their phones?
10. What technology could you not live without?

### 自然与天气

1. What is the weather like in your country?
2. What is your favourite season and why?
3. Do you enjoy being outdoors?
4. Have you ever experienced extreme weather?
5. Do you think the climate in your country is changing?
6. Do you prefer hot or cold weather?
7. Do you enjoy spending time in nature?
8. Have you ever been to a national park or nature reserve?
9. Do you think people should do more to protect the environment?
10. Does the weather affect your mood?

### 健康与运动

1. Do you do any sport or exercise?
2. How important is physical fitness to you?
3. Do you think you have a healthy lifestyle?
4. What do you do to stay healthy?
5. Have you ever had a sports injury?
6. Do you prefer team sports or individual sports?
7. Do you watch sport on television?
8. Do you think schools should focus more on physical education?
9. How do you deal with stress?
10. Do you get enough sleep?

### 节日与庆典

1. What is your favourite holiday or festival?
2. How do you usually celebrate important festivals?
3. Are festivals important in your culture?
4. Do you think modern people celebrate festivals differently from previous generations?
5. Is there a festival from another culture you would like to experience?

## Part 3 话题（深度讨论类）共120题

Part 3 考官就 Part 2 话题延伸提问，要求考生表达观点、分析原因、预测未来。

### 人际关系与社会

1. How have relationships between people changed in modern society?
2. Do you think people today are less sociable than in the past?
3. What role does family play in shaping a person's character?
4. Is it more difficult to make friends as an adult than as a child?
5. How important is it to maintain friendships over a long period?
6. Do you think social media strengthens or weakens personal relationships?
7. How has the role of women in the family changed in recent decades?
8. Do you think people are becoming more or less tolerant of others?
9. How important is community spirit in modern cities?
10. Do you think individualism is increasing in modern societies?

### 教育与学习

1. How do you think education will change in the next 20 years?
2. Do you think university education is necessary for success?
3. What are the advantages and disadvantages of studying abroad?
4. Should schools focus more on preparing students for work or for life?
5. Do you think children today face more academic pressure than in the past?
6. How important is it for schools to teach values and ethics?
7. Do you think traditional or progressive teaching methods are more effective?
8. How has technology changed the way people learn?
9. Do you think lifelong learning is important? Why?
10. What are the main challenges facing education systems today?

### 科技与现代生活

1. How has technology changed daily life over the past 20 years?
2. Do you think people are too dependent on technology?
3. What are the risks of artificial intelligence for society?
4. How will automation affect the job market in the future?
5. Do you think social media has more positive or negative effects?
6. How can governments regulate technology companies effectively?
7. Do you think the internet has made people better or worse informed?
8. What impact has technology had on human creativity?
9. Do you think privacy is still possible in the digital age?
10. How might technology change the nature of work in the future?

### 环境与自然

1. Who do you think bears the greatest responsibility for addressing climate change?
2. Do you think individuals can make a meaningful difference to the environment?
3. How effective are international agreements on environmental issues?
4. Should economic development be sacrificed for environmental protection?
5. Do you think future generations will inherit a better or worse planet?
6. How can cities become more environmentally sustainable?
7. Do you think renewable energy will completely replace fossil fuels?
8. What can governments do to encourage more sustainable consumption?
9. How important is environmental education for young people?
10. Do you think animals should have the same rights as humans?

### 工作与经济

**备考建议**

Part 1 准备 2—3 句流畅自然的回答即可，不要背稿。Part 2 用 STAR 结构组织（Situation / Task / Action / Result），保证逻辑清晰。Part 3 是拉开分数的关键，需要表达观点、给出理由、举例论证，每个回答至少3—4句，体现思维深度。
